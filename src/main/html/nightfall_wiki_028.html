Tahlkora insists that she overheard Varesh and General Kahyet plotting to weaken Istan. You infiltrate the corsairs to investigate these claims and make your way through the bogs to rendezvous with Kahyet and prevent a deal from going ahead.

Mission information[edit]

Requirements[edit]

  • Tahlkora must be in your party.

Objectives[edit]

Find General Kahyet. Prevent her from striking a deal with Ironfist, the corsair captain.

  • ADDED: Eliminate Ironfist's envoy and crew.
  • ADDED: Steal the corsair garb.
  • ADDED: Pass through the Kournan check point.
  • ADDED: Follow Captain Besuz to the meeting with Kahyet.
  • ADDED: Kill Kahyet.
  • *BONUS* Clear Lahtenda Bog of rinkhal monitors.
  • You have [5...0] of 5 rinkhal monitors remaining.

Walkthrough[edit]

Lahtenda Bog is a maze but Captain Besuz will show you the way once you find him. Turn right at the first opportunity to find Ironfist's Envoy - kill the corsair group to take their clothes as a disguise (non-human party members will not be disguised). Head southeast until you find Captain Besuz's roadblock. Talk to the captain to continue, he will now lead you to the rendezvous point with General Kahyet. Follow him and defeat the various groups of Skale on your path. You will soon see a camp and a narrow path where a stampede of Skale will run through, use it as a choke point. As you get closer to the ruins, a group of Harpies will spawn too. Once you reach the rendezvous, General Kahyet will arrive and trigger a brief conversation; kill her to complete the mission.

Bonus[edit]

The bonus requires the party to kill 5 Rinkhal Monitors, a simple but time consuming task.

  1. The first is south of Ironfist's Envoy.
  2. The second is after Captain Besuz's roadblock and to the east, inside a natural cave with ancient relics.
  3. The third is very close to the second, just northeast of it.
    Now return to Besuz who will wait for you to proceed. Follow him until you pass the three named corsairs and then when Besuz turns left, you turn right instead to find the last two Rinkhal Monitors.
  4. On the North side of the swamp.
  5. North from target 4 surrounded by Mandragor and Skale.

Finally backtrack to rejoin Besuz and finish the mission.

Skill recommendations[edit]

  • Condition-removal skills since foes cause disease frequently (e.g. Extinguish). In Hard mode, the Motivation Henchman Sogolon has Cautery Signet.
  • Area of effect damage is useful during the Skale stampede.

Hard mode[edit]

When you reach the Corsair camp, use the top of the hill to flag heroes/henchmen before the Skale stampede begins, this provides height advantage. The Skale mob partially separates making them easier to kill.

Alternatively it is possible to head northwest before encountering the Skale stampede, followed by heading south and carefully pulling the two separate groups apart with a long or flatbow.

Notes[edit]

  • If you talk to One-Eyed Rugger (one of the 3 corsair NPCs), you will become drunk.
    • The warped-screen effect persists until you complete the mission; it does not contribute to the Drunkard title nor contribute towards skills that benefit from being drunk.
    • You can remove the screen-warping by disabling the "Enable post-process effects" in the options panel.
  • Mapping the mission area (Lahtenda Bog) will contribute ~2.5% towards the Elona cartographer title.
  • At the completion of this mission, you could have up to eight party members in Sunspear Great Hall (exceeding that outpost's normal party size); you can use this to vanquish the Plains of Jarin.
    • Your party will consist of its leader, the required hero (Tahlkora), and up to six others: players, heroes (maximum of 3 per player will be teleported), or henchmen (however, only Kihm, Herta, Sogolon, and Gehraz will show up; the other henchmen do not appear in the great hall).
    • An easier solution is to ask Dockmaster Dimedeh to teleport your party of 8 from Dajkah Inlet to Kamadan, Jewel of Istan and vanquish from there.
Bug. It is possible to pass through some of the Kournan Guard barriers before speaking to Captain Besuz. There is no benefit in doing so and requires more time than finishing the mission normally.
Bug. Occasionally, General Kahyet and her group will appear, but fail to turn hostile. There is no work-around; you must restart the mission.

Trivia[edit]
