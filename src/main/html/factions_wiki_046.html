Quest information[edit]

Objectives[edit]

  • Travel to House zu Heltzer. [sic]
  • Speak with Guardsman Oldrich at Altrumm Ruins.
  • See Count zu Heltzer for your reward.

Reward[edit]

  • 4,000 Experience
  • 250 Kurzick Faction
  • 1 Skill point

Walkthrough[edit]

Once you've obtained this quest from Jamei, simply map-travel over to the Altrumm Ruins and speak with Guardsman Oldrich. He will teleport your party into House zu Heltzer, where you can collect your reward from Count zu Heltzer.

Dialogue[edit]

Initial dialogue[edit]

Jamei

Master Togo wanted me to give you some special instructions before you visit the Kurzicks. You should know that above all, the Kurzicks respect creation above all else. Additionally, everything the Kurzicks do must first be approved by the council of nobles. I suggest that you use diplomacy and show respect to the Kurzick nobles. Speak with Count zu Heltzer in House zu Heltzer first. He has a great deal of influence and might be sympathetic to our cause. To find him, speak with Guardsman Oldrich in Altrumm Ruins .[sic] This will allow access to House zu Heltzer.

 Accept: "I'll seek out the count."
 Decline: "I haven't decided yet."
 Ask: "You know, I really have better things to do than to stand around here repeating the same information over and over again. One last time: If you hope to sway the Kurzicks, you will need to go to House zu Heltzer in Echovald Forest and speak with Count zu Heltzer."

Reward dialogue[edit]

Count zu Heltzer

I thought Shiro had been dealt with using the sacred Urn of Saint Viktor? Master Togo's plan must have failed, then. Well, in any case, I cannot move the Kurzicks on the word of a stranger, and certainly the Redemptors will need to meditate on the matter and divine the will of the gods. If you truly wish to help in this effort, you must first prove yourself a friend. Once you have done so, I'll secure you an audience with the Council of Nobles.
