The Vehtendi Valley contains one of a few fertile stretches of the river Elon within the borders of Vabbi. Farmlands and settlements are concentrated towards the south. In the northeastern corner, across the river, lies the Hanging Gardens of Jahin.

Getting there[edit]

Characters will first arrive in this area as part of the primary quest Rally The Princes, traveling from Yatendi Canyons from the southwest.

Notes[edit]

  • Complete exploration of this area contributes approximately 2.9% to the Elonian Cartographer title.
  • Vanquisher runs of this area without active quests requires defeating around 250 foes. Including quest influences, it has been reported to range from 241 to 259.
    • The quest Rally The Princes reduces the number of foes and removes the bosses Bohdalz the Furious and Jarimiya the Unmerciful.
    • Starting the vanquish from Yahnur Market minimises the amount of backtracking to clear out the area on the other side of the outpost.
    • Be careful when vanquishing the southwestern area of the map. Four boss groups are relatively close to each other.
  • This is one of the few areas in Guild Wars where the ambient environment of the area resembles nighttime.
