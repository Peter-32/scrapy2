Kaineng Docks

Path from Kaineng Center to Kaineng Docks

Kaineng Docks is the primary dock area where people travel to and from Seitung Harbor on Shing Jea Island.

Getting there[edit]

Talk to First Mate Xiang in Seitung Harbor or exit from The Marketplace.

Exits[edit]

  • The Marketplace (west)
  • Seitung Harbor (east) (By talking to First Mate Xiang)

Shrines[edit]

Resurrection shrines with a statue
  • Melandru's Watcher
    • At the docks

Quests available[edit]

(quest is only available to Factions characters)
  • Headmaster Greico
    • Mayhem in the Market
