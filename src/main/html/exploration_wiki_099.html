The Nightfallen Jahai is a dark mockery of Jahai Bluffs in Kourna. Even the Elon River is mirrored; in its place is the River of Souls. The Sun is black and twisted. This area is outside the Gate of Torment and is the first explorable area in the Realm of Torment that your party will traverse.

Getting there[edit]

The primary quest Uncharted Territory, available from the Gate of Torment, will continue the storyline and lead the party across this area.

Many secondary quests from the Gate of Torment take place here.

Notes[edit]

  • While in this area, your party suffers from the Edge of Reason environment effect (i.e. skills take an additional 3 seconds to recharge).
  • This area does not contribute to the Elonian Cartographer title since it is in the Realm of Torment and does not show up on the world map.
  • This area is not required for the Elonian Vanquisher title.
  • To the north you can find an alternate version of the Monument to the Defeat of Palawa Joko. When you step into its opened mouth, you might not be able to get out if bodyblocked by Kormir, meaning you'll have to re-zone to escape.
  • Completing the quest The Missing Patrol in this area will start the quest The Oddbodies, granting you 5 powerful allies that will follow you around the map. They're great company when you're farming Lightbringer points.
