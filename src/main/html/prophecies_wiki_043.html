To Kryta: Refugees
Section Primary Quests
Campaign Prophecies

(Tyrian only)

Given by Master Saberlin
in Beacon's Perch
(Northern Shiverpeaks)
Preceded by The Frost Gate
Followed by To Kryta: The Ice Cave
Type Primary quest
Map(s)

Path to Ranger Olson from Beacon's Perch

Start a chain of quests leading the Ascalonian Refugees to Kryta.

Quest information[edit]

Requirements[edit]

  • Tyrian (Prophecies) character

Objectives[edit]

  • Eliminate the [6...0] remaining Centaur patrol[s] along the main road.
  • See Ranger Olson for your reward.

Reward[edit]

  • 500 Experience

Dialogue[edit]

Initial dialogue[edit]

Master Saberlin
We've encountered Centaur patrols on the road ahead, and they're attacking the caravan! I need you to take them out quickly and report to Ranger Olson.
 Accept: "You can count on me."
 Decline: "I don't know if I can handle that."
 Ask: "Follow the main road and take care of the Centaur patrols! When the way is clear, report to Ranger Olson."

Reward dialogue[edit]

Ranger Olson
We're fortunate to have your assistance. Aidan has gone on ahead into the ice caves to scout the way for you to take the refugees through. Master Seeker Nathaniel awaits you in Griffon's Mouth with further instructions.

Notes[edit]

  • You don't actually need to save any refugees, you just need to kill the centaur groups to complete the quest.
  • The next quest in the line isn't given by Ranger Olson, but by Master Seeker Nathaniel in Griffon's Mouth.
