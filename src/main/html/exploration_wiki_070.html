Majesty's Rest is a remote area that marks the transition between Kryta and the Maguuma Jungle. One of the game's most powerful foes, Rotscale, makes his home outside The Mausoleum. The Scepter of Orr was hidden in one of the buildings within this area until the White Mantle recovered it.

Getting there[edit]

From Kryta, either leave from Temple of the Ages and head west through the Black Curtain and Talmark Wilderness or from Fisherman's Haven and travel north through Stingray Strand and Tears of the Fallen, then northwest through Talmark Wilderness.

From the Maguuma Jungle, leave from the Wilds or Druid's Overlook and head east/northeast (respectively) through Sage Lands.

Notes[edit]

  • Interactive objects
    • Krytan Chest
    • Statue of Balthazar
    • Enter the Mausoleum (only during Commandeering a Mortal Vessel)
  • Complete exploration of this area contributes approximately 0.9% to the Tyrian Cartographer title.
  • Vanquisher runs of this area without active quests requires defeating around 65 foes. Including quest influences, it has been reported to range from 44 to 85.
    • If you avoid triggering too many popups you can realistically complete the vanquish with around 55 kills. All the visible enemies are concentrated on the eastern half, so it is recommended to start from Temple of the Ages and approach from Talmark Wilderness.
    • This explorable area is home to Rotscale.
    • The popups on the eastern side include Reed Stalkers in the water, hidden Grasping Ghouls near undead spawns, and one or two spots with Fog Nightmares and Smoke Phantoms.
    • The western side has close to a dozen spots of hidden spiders (where there are trees overhead) and devourers (in the dry brown areas).
    • Although Majesty’s Rest is considered to be in Kryta, it is divided into a Maguuma Jungle area and a Krytan area, each with their own typical vegetation, landscape and foes. Chests in the Maguuma Jungle area will be Krytan Chests.
    • Your party gains three Mad King's Guards as allies during Commandeering a Mortal Vessel.
