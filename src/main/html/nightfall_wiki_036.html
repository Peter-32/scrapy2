Quest information[edit]

Objectives[edit]

  • The Princes of Vabbi await you in the Halls of Chokhin.
  • Defeat the invading forces and protect the messenger of Lyssa.
  • Speak with the messenger of Lyssa.
  • Hurry to the Mirror of Lyss and investigate what's happening at the Grand Court of Sebelkeh.
  • Listen to Morgahn's explanation.
  • See Head Priest Vahmani for your reward.

Reward[edit]

  • 2,000 Experience
  • 250 Gold
  • 10 Lightbringer Points

Walkthrough[edit]

If you have Mihanu Township unlocked, you can map travel there and head north to the Hall of Chokhin; however if you don't, it is easiest to leave from the Grand Court of Sebelkeh outpost and head west to Mihanu Township.

After you walk near the princes, the quest evolves. You have to protect the Messenger of Lyssa against the four Margonites. After you talked to the messenger, map to the Grand Court of Sebelkeh and enter The Mirror of Lyss through the eastern exit. After talking to Morgahn, you see a cinematic. Now you can enter the Grand Court of Sebelkeh mission.

Notes[edit]

  • If the Messenger of Lyssa dies at any point - even after talking to her - you will have to restart the quest.
Anomaly. During this quest, you can find another Prince Mehtu standing in the left chamber of the halls, in addition to the Prince Mehtu that spawns for the quest.
Anomaly. It is possible to skip the first three objectives by talking to Morgahn.
Anomaly. When doing this quest in Hard Mode, Vabbi Guards are occasionally able to outheal the damage output from the Margonites if a Margonite Cleric is present, causing them to never reach the Halls of Chokhin.
