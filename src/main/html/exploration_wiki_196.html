Stingray Strand is a beautiful stretch of idyllic beach on the western side of Kryta, that is unfortunately marred by the presence of hostile Tengu and dangerous Skale.

Notes[edit]

  • The Royal Papers quest will affect the spawns in this area.
  • Large Spined Aloes popup throughout the area.
  • Complete exploration of this area contributes approximately 0.94% to the Tyrian Cartographer title.
  • Vanquisher runs of this area without active quests require defeating around 150 foes. Including quest influences, the number of foes has been reported to range from 150 to 170.
    • The Inferno Imps deal AoE fire damage and cause Burning; they can be countered by keeping spread out and by using general anti-caster techniques and/or party healing.
    • The Caromi Tengu include warriors, rangers, and mesmers; bring melee counters for the first two and be careful of the AoE interrupts of the Caromi Wilds.
    • The Bog Skales use health degeneration and corpse exploitation. Consider bringing Interrupts and hex removal to use against them. A minion master can help reduce the available corpses for exploitation.

Trivia[edit]

  • This is one of the few areas in Kryta which has no changes during the War in Kryta storyline.
