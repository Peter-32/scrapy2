The Boreas Seabed refers to the northernmost shores of the Jade Sea and its surrounding areas. There are plenty of Naga and Outcasts found on the frozen waves. The eastern end of this area is the location of the Luxons' Convocation.

Getting there[edit]

This area is right outside the Zos Shivros Channel outpost. It is a mostly optional area, but it does have several secondary quests taking place in it.

Shrines[edit]

Resurrection shrines with bounties
  • Luxon Priest → Blessing of the Luxons
    • At the exit into Zos Shivros Channel.
Resurrection shrines with a statue
  • Champion of Balthazar
    • Northeast, at the foot of the path leading to the 1,000 Daggers guild hall.
  • Lyssa's Muse
    • South, at the north exit into Zos Shivros Channel.
  • Melandru's Watcher
    • Southwest, at the exit into Mount Qinkai.

Notes[edit]

  • These quests affect the spawns in this area:
    • Protect the Halcyon
    • The Halcyon Job
  • Vanquisher runs of this area without active quests requires defeating around 219 foes. Including quest influences, it has been reported to range from 158 to 237.
    • There are 4 hidden Oni groups (each with 3 or 4 of them):
      • Just south of the northern central beach.
      • South of the beach where Sskai, Dragon's Birth is located.
      • West of the beach where Sskai, Dragon's Birth is located.
      • At the foot of the path leading up to the 1,000 Daggers guild hall.
  • The Naga and Outcasts will fight with each other. Waiting until they are done leaves you with significantly fewer foes, making a vanquisher run easier because you won't have to fight large groups anymore.
Bug.On the bridge leading to the 1,000 Daggers guild hall, there are "blind spots" where allies and foes can become invisible. This error is also seen in Xaquang Skyway.
