Notes[edit]

The Guild Wars 2 Wiki has an article on Skyward Reach.
  • These quests will affect the spawns in this area:
    • Into the Unknown
  • Complete exploration of this area adds approximately 1.33% to the Tyrian Cartographer title.
  • Vanquisher runs of this area have been reported to require defeating 162 to 178 foes, including quest influences.
    • Hydra are found more frequently here than anywhere else in the Crystal Desert so skills like Protective Spirit, Shelter, Spirit Bond, and Mantra of Flame are highly recommended.
    • There are a dozen Desert Griffons in the fingertip of land south of Augury Rock.
    • Near Diviner's Ascent, there are a couple of spots with hidden Rockshot Devourers.
    • There is also a short patrol of Enchanted near the Vulture Drifts portal.
  • The zone includes two landmarks with no known function:
    • A large skeleton (of an unknown creature) rests near Mourn Drakespur.
    • Just outside Destiny's Gorge is a spot with four pedestals, each with a ramp leading up to it.
