This is the transition area that brings players from Guild Wars Nightfall into Guild Wars Eye of the North.

Getting there[edit]

Enter the fissure that spawns in the Plains of Jarin during the Hole of Istan quest or after the completion of that quest, What Lies Beneath or I Feel the Earth Move Under Cantha's Feet.

Quests available[edit]

  • The Beginning of the End (automatically acquired after the Encounter in the Depths cinematic)
