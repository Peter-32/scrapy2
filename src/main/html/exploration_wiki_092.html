Scarred Earth
Campaign Prophecies
Region The Mists
Type Explorable area
Exit(s) The Courtyard
Images

Map of Scarred Earth.

Parties must defeat all of the enemies to advance to the Courtyard.

Getting there[edit]

You arrive in this area after defeating all of the enemies in the Underworld.

NPCs[edit]

Foes[edit]

Demons (Torment creatures)

  • 28 (30) Grasp of Insanity
  • 24 (26) Scythe of Chaos

Dryders (Underworld army)

  • 28 (30) Terrorweb Dryder

Ghosts (Underworld army)

  • 28 (30) Banished Dream Rider

Wurms

  • 30 (32) Chaos Wurm

Zombies (Underworld army)

  • 22 (26) Chained Soul

Notes[edit]

  • There are no resurrection shrines here; a party wipe here returns your party to the outpost.
