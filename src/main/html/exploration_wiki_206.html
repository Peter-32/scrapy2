The sprawling compound of Minister Cho's Estate encompasses a large part of central Shing Jea Island, taking up most of the land to the north of the central mountain range. The estate grounds have mostly been turned into a zoo of sorts, home to a myriad variety of exotic animals from all over the world of Tyria.

Getting there[edit]

The only entrance is from Ran Musu Gardens.

Shrines[edit]

Resurrection shrines with a statue
  • Lyssa's Muse
    • Near the center along the northern beach.
  • Melandru's Watcher
    • West, at the exit into Ran Musu Gardens.
  • Voice of Grenth
    • East, near the exit into Minister Cho's Estate.

Notes[edit]

  • These quests affect the spawns in this area: Lost Treasure and Rescue at Minister Cho's Estate.
  • Complete exploration of this area will yield approximately 2.6% to the Canthan Cartographer title track.
  • Vanquisher runs of this area without active quests requires defeating around 210 foes. Including quest influences, it has been reported to range from 192 to 220.
    • There are no bosses in this area, avoid skills that recharge with Morale Boost (i.e. Resurrection Signet and Sunspear Rebirth Signet).
    • Since you can only have a party of four, having a spirit spammer helps.
    • There is a chain of islands to the northeast with foes that cannot easily be seen from the beach. Likewise, there are more foes inside the caverns to the southeast.
