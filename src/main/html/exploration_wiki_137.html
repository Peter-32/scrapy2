Vulture Drifts is dotted with ruins of a failed civilization, overcome by the unforgiving nature of the Crystal Desert. In the northwest, a circular series of strange monuments, perhaps tombs, surrounding a large skeleton of an unknown creature.

Notes[edit]

The Guild Wars 2 Wiki has an article on Vulture Drifts.
  • Complete exploration of this area contributes approximately 1.9% to the Tyrian Cartographer title.
  • Condition removal helps counter Plague Touch or Eruption and "Can't Touch This!" blocks the touch skills of scarabs.


Vanquishing[edit]

  • Vanquisher runs of this area without active quests require defeating around 230 foes. Including quest influences, it has been reported to range from 207 to 261.
    • There are a lot of hidden scarabs towards the northwest, especially near the northwestern corner. Move slowly to avoid over-aggroing.
    • There are a few hidden devourers and possibly Sand Wurms in the southeastern and eastern sections.
    • The western section can be difficult due the knock downs from Sand Elementals and Sand Drakes; pull carefully, bring anti-KD skills, or, at least, keep your party spread out.
    • A lone Hydra group patrols the northwest and is easily missed because it starts circling clockwise when you enter its radar range. To avoid hunting for it, approach from the east when you first encounter it (so that it moves towards you); if you think you missed it, start your own patrol in a counter-clockwise direction. The group does not always spawn.
    • Two groups of Sand Giants patrol the Eastern side of the zone.
  • Expect to spend 10 minutes finding the last foes on this map as its very vast and annoying to navigate.
  • Bring "Can't Touch This!" or "Don't Trip!": touch skills and AoE knockdown are abundant!
