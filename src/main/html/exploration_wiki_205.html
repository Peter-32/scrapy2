Map of The Courtyard.

The Courtyard is the area prior to the the Hall of Heroes and the third map in the Ruins of the Tomb of the Primeval Kings.

Getting there[edit]

You arrive in this area after defeating all of the enemies in Scarred Earth.

NPCs[edit]

Foes[edit]

Demons (Torment creatures)

  • 28 (30) Grasp of Insanity
  • 24 (26) Scythe of Chaos

Dryders (Underworld army)

  • 28 (30) Terrorweb Dryder

Ghosts (Underworld army)

  • 28 (30) Banished Dream Rider

Wurms

  • 30 (32) Chaos Wurm

Zombies (Underworld army)

  • 22 (26) Chained Soul

Notes[edit]

  • As with any elite area, there are no resurrection shrines; a party wipe here returns your party to the outpost.
