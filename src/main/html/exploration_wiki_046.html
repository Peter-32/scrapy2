The Ascalon Foothills slopes up to the west to meet the cold sides of the Shiverpeaks. It is a small area populated by elementals and hydra, one of the most dangerous foes in the Ascalon region to the ill-prepared.

Getting there[edit]

Leave from Yak's Bend southeast through Traveler's Vale or from Nolani Academy or Grendich Courthouse and travel north/west (respectively) through Diessa Lowlands.

Shrines[edit]

Resurrection shrines
  • Northwest, at the exit into Traveler's Vale.
  • Southeast, at the exit into Diessa Lowlands.
Statue of Grenth
  • North, along the edge where the terrain turns to snow.

Notes[edit]

  • Interactive objects
    • Oswalt's Headstone
  • Complete exploration of this area contributes approximately 0.5% to the Tyrian Cartographer title.
  • Vanquisher runs of this area without active quests requires defeating around 55 foes. Including quest influences, it has been reported to range from 51 to 59.
    • If starting from Yak's Bend or Borlis Pass, you can come to this area with a party size of 6. Consider continuing onwards into Diessa Lowlands with the larger group.
