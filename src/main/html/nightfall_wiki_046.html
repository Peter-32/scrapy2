Consulate Docks is the first mission in the Nightfall campaign for characters from other campaigns. You join the Sunspears as they assault Gandara, the Moon Fortress in an attempt to depose Varesh and end her threat to Elona. Make sure you have Dunkoro in your party to enter.

Mission information[edit]

Requirements[edit]

You need to have Dunkoro in your party to enter this mission.

Objectives[edit]

Assault Gandara.

  • Unite the western and southern attack fronts.
  • Capture the forward wall!
  • ADDED: Defeat the Kournan Gate Captain to prevent him [sic] from summoning Kournan troops.
  • ADDED: Kill the siege engineers manning the firespitters to clear the way for the southern attack front.
  • ADDED: Capture the Kournan bombard to gain entrance to the heart of Gandara.
  • ADDED: Rally the attack force outside the Plaza of the Five Gods.
  • *BONUS* Eliminate the Kournan field marshal groups.
  • You have [3...0] of 3 Kournan field marshal groups remaining.

Walkthrough[edit]

For this mission only, all party members will be buffed with Sunspear Battle Call, which increases maximum health and armor (but not attribute points) to the equivalent of a level 20 character. An effect that helps low level characters/heroes to get through the mission more easily.

Leave the boat and start to attack. You will encounter several Kournan Bowmen, who tend to focus their arrows on one single target, making them deadly to unprepared players. Spellcaster characters should try to attack them from behind walls to cover from their projectiles.

Once you've killed the archers, go down to the western gate guarded by Captain Lumanda who is able to call in reinforcements and get rid of her to prevent additional Kournan soldier spawns.

In the next area, your reinforcements will be obstructed by two firespitters. Kill the Kournan Engineers and their groups to turn them off and reform the army by speaking to Nerashi. Make sure your allies do not remain stuck behind ground obstacles (or you will lose offense).

Continue forward, killing Kournans to capture the bombard which can be used to breach the fortress. Captain Mwende and four Elite Kournans will be stationed behind the gate.

Climb the stairs to reach the Plaza of the Five Gods, where Varesh is trapped by the invasion to finish the mission.

Bonus[edit]

To gain the bonus rewards, your party has to kill three additional Kournan Field Commanders and their groups. These Commanders make use of a skill called Kournan Siege, which deals massive damage and also interrupts actions.

  1. The first is near mission start, at the top left stairs and before Captain Lumanda.
  2. The second is situated past the Kournan bombard weapon and can be found after the cinematic but before Captain Mwende.
  3. The third and final is after Captain Mwende but before the Plaza of the Five Gods. Do not proceed to engage Varesh; instead, detour northeast to find them, otherwise you might accidentally finish the mission before you're able to complete the bonus.
Bug.The first Field Commander may sometimes spawn beyond the closed gate. If you do not have any ranged spell attacks, you may need to restart the mission to get the Master's reward.

Hard mode[edit]

Avoid over-aggro especially around the main gate and third Field Commander.

Skill recommendations[edit]

  • Skills that remove stances, bypass blocking or punish blocking to counter the Whirling Defense skill used by the Kournan Bowmen.
  • Skills which defend against ranged attacks (e.g., "Shields Up!"), or provide damage reduction over time (e.g., Shielding Hands).
  • All foes are fleshy, so there will be many corpses for minion masters; Lonai is also a minion master.
  • Enchantment-based builds are vulnerable to enchantment removal by Kournan Oppressors and Seers.
  • Hex removal skills could be unnecessary; the only hex spells your party will encounter are Enchanter's Conundrum (the hex that is applied has no impact if your party does not use enchantments) and Life Siphon (ends when its caster dies).

Notes[edit]

The "Demon" group just before the cinematic.
  • Kormir follows the party leader, never attacks and rarely uses skills except for "Incoming!". Foes cannot target her but she does take damage from area of effect skills.
  • Firespitters will kill allies immediately, but it does not count as a death.
  • As mentioned in the walkthrough, if you attempt to take the narrow passage between the firespitters and the wall, Nerashi, Rojis, and Lonia can become stuck and not follow you. This can be indicated by their names becoming gray in your Party Members list when you've run too far away. If this is the case, go back, regroup with them, and take the path around the wall.
  • After this mission, nearly every single boss has an elite skill.
  • After the completion of this mission for the first time, Koss will become unavailable until the completion of The Great Escape quest.
  • After completing this mission, your party will be taken to Yohlon Haven.
