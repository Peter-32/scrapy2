Quest information[edit]

Requirements[edit]

  • Elonian (Nightfall) character

Objectives[edit]

  • Make your way to Chahbek Village.
  • Speak to First Spear Jahdugar.
  • See First Spear Jahdugar for your reward.

Reward[edit]

  • 2,000 Experience
  • Koss joins your party.

Walkthrough[edit]

Escort Kormir, Koss and 3 Sunspear Recruits to First Spear Jahdugar just outside Chahbek Village. Along the way, 2 Corsairs will popup, however they are easily defeated.

Dialogue[edit]

Initial dialogue[edit]

Kormir
If we take this route to Chahbek Village, we can hope for no support from our watch post nearby. It is faster, but you must be sure of yourself and your abilities if we hope to have any chance of success.
 Accept: "I am confident in my abilities."
 Decline: "I must reconsider this."
 Ask: "We must hurry! Chahbek Village is in great danger!"

Intermediate dialogue[edit]

Koss: That's the spirit, [Character Name]! Recruits, ready your weapons and prepare for battle by pressing "I" to open your inventory. Then, double-click on the weapon the Spearmarshall has given you.
Koss: Here we go! It's a short jaunt through these shallows to Chahbek Village.
Kormir: [Character Name]! Take point! Koss and I will back you up.
Kormir: These corsairs must be a scouting party for the main force. Let's hope they haven't already overwhelmed the village defenses.... (bubble)
Koss: Corsair scum! (bubble)
First Spear Jahdugar

Thank the gods you've arrived! The corsair vanguard is overwhelming our defenses.

Let me know when you are ready.
Let me know when you are ready!
We are ready. (sent to Chahbek Village (cinematics)#Cinematic 1)
We are not ready yet.

Reward dialogue[edit]

First Spear Jahdugar

Now that you're here we can drive those blasted corsairs out of the village!

Notes[edit]

  • Accepting the quest unlocks three skills for the character to use.

Resurrection Signet, and 2 profession skills as follows:

Healing Signet Power Attack
Power Shot Troll Unguent
Orison of Healing Bane Signet
Deathly Swarm Vampiric Gaze
Ether Feast Empathy
Aura of Restoration Flare
Leader's Comfort Spear of Lightning
Vital Boon Pious Assault
  • Resigning after accepting this quest will cause you to move to the outpost even if you haven't completed any of the objectives.
