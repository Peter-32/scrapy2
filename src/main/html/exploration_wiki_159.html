Nebo Terrace is a well populated province in north-central Kryta. There are settlements along all the edges and a central village, also named Nebo. In the southwest, undead from the swamps are making incursions.

Getting there[edit]

Head east out of Beetletun or north from Bergen Hot Springs.

  • Southwest, by the exit to Cursed Lands.
Statue of Dwayna
  • Northeast, just south of Beldon the Blade and west of the exit into North Kryta Province.

Notes[edit]

The Guild Wars 2 Wiki has an article on Nebo Terrace.
  • In the southern area, each undead boss has approximately a 3.3% chance to spawn (there are 6 possible bosses and only about a 20% chance that any of them appear).
  • This a popular farm for Decayed Orr Emblems (they can be traded for festive items during Halloween and Wintersday).
  • Complete exploration of this area contributes 1.17% to the Tyrian Cartographer title.
  • Vanquisher runs of this area without active quests require defeating around 150 foes. Including quest influences, it has been reported to range from 138 to 226.
    • There are few exploitable corpses in the southern area, but plenty elsewhere.
    • A party of eight can caravan vanquish the area by leaving from the Temple of the Ages.
    • There are hidden Spined Aloes in the northwestern area.
    • During Mustering a Response, Captain Langmar and Lieutenant Thackeray will join you, but the challenge will be more difficult: there will be closer to 235 foes; the extra 50% will be mostly White Mantle with the tougher War in Kryta builds.
