The Wilderness of Bahdza appears to be the eastern border of Vabbi, with the Citadel of Dzagon, that resides in the northwest of the area, under the command of Prince Ahmtur the Mighty providing the crucial defense against the hostile inhabitants of this frontier. The fortifications cover a relatively large portion of the northwestern corner of this area.

Getting there[edit]

This optional area can be easily reached by heading out the eastern exit of the Dzagonur Bastion mission outpost.

Notes[edit]

  • Complete exploration of this area contributes approximately 2.95% to the Elonian Cartographer title.
  • Spawns in this area permanently change after certain quests are completed.
  • Vanquisher runs of this area without active quests requires defeating around 170 foes. Including quest influences, it has been reported to range from 156 to 176.
    • Completion of all the local quests will reduce the foe count by dozens of Harpies and Hekets. However, completing Destroy the Harpies causes Korrub, Flame of Dreams to spawn, who is considered to be one of the more difficult foes to defeat.
    • There are a few hidden beetle groups in the southeast, some of which overlap patrol paths of other beetle groups and behemoth groups.
    • After Scratcher or Screecher die, several harpy groups spawn and run towards the Resplendent Makuun exit. These new foes will not attack the party and they do not need to be killed to vanquish the area, unless they get stuck.
  • The Minotaur Hunt bounty will be offered even though there are no minotaurs in the area until after completing Extinction.
