North Kryta Province is, as the name suggests, a large province in northern Kryta. It is also the location of the Ascalon Settlement.

Getting there[edit]

Head north from Lion's Arch or D'Alessio Seaboard.

Shrines[edit]

Resurrection shrines
  • Northwest corner, on the path to the Ascalon Settlement, near the west entrance.
  • Northeast corner, on the path from the entrance from Scoundrel's Rise.
  • Southern half, approximately half-way between the entrances to Lion's Arch and D'Alessio Seaboard, on the path running north.
  • South-southeast, just outside the entrance to Lion's Arch.
  • Southwest corner, just outside the entrance from D'Alessio Seaboard.

Dialogue[edit]

Outside Lion's Arch, prior to completion of The Ascalon Settlement:

Cynn: "It's a good thing you've got a job as a merchant because you make a terrible tour guide."
Damaris: "I am doing my best to help you find the Ascalon Settlement."
Mhenlo: "Cynn, we only just arrived in Kryta. Please don't get us thrown out already."
Cynn: "I'm just saying, I could get better directions from an imp."
Aidan: "Though I doubt you could get us thrown out of an entire country, I would prefer you not try, Cynn."
Devona: "I am sorry for my companion's rudeness, Damaris. Please continue."
Damaris: "It's quite all right. You'll find the Ascalon Settlement to the northwest."
Damaris: "It's not far from the shores of the Giant's Basin...the great salt lake out past the swamp."

Notes[edit]

  • Complete exploration of this area contributes approximately 1.6% to the Tyrian Cartographer title.
  • Vanquisher runs of this area without active quests require defeating around 185 foes. Including quest influences, it has been reported to range from 126 to 241.
    • More foes during Graven Images.
    • Defend North Kryta Province (Hard Mode) in conjunction with War in Kryta spawns may however, accounting for the completion of the quest and hidden spawns all over the area, drive the enemy count up to 300.
    • Elemental damage protection can help against the Fire and Inferno Imps. Remember to spread out before engaging them.
    • Prophecies characters who have not completed the Ascalon Settlement can have Mhenlo, Cynn, Devona, and Aidan help vanquish the area. (They will be level 20 but possess no elite skills.)
