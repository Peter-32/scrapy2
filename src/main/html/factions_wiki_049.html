Quest information[edit]

Objectives[edit]

  • Recover the stolen chest of turtle eggs.
  • Return the chest of turtle eggs to Hatchery Keeper Gratian.
  • See Endre for your reward.

Reward[edit]

  • 4,000 Experience
  • 1,250 Luxon Faction
  • 150 Gold
  • 1 Skill Point

Walkthrough[edit]

From Cavalon follow the path on the mission map east then north. The chest is located in the southwest corner of Maishang Hills, point 1 on the mission map. Sergeant Rodrik and the Outcast crew will start a conversation once you get close, and soon will become hostile and attack. After the fight is over, grab the chest and follow the path marked on the mission map. After crossing the bridge at point 2, you will need to turn almost 180 degrees to the left and take the path heading south. Take the chest to Gyala Hatchery which is in the southeast corner of the same territory. The Kappa and Naga you encounter along this path are quite strong so come prepared. Also as you approach the beach at the end, you will run into a group of Naga which includes Stsou Swiftscale.

The chest is a bundle item, which provides no blessing of any kind, but does not interfere with movement speed.

Initial dialogue[edit]

Elder Rhea
"You have proven yourself a capable and loyal ally of the Luxons, so it is with confidence that I impart on you this most vital mission. You have, of course, seen how important the great turtles are to us, both as beasts of burden and beasts of war. Unfortunately, a group of bandits have recently attacked one of our supply caravans and stolen a chest of turtle eggs before they could reach the hatchery. Our turtles are very rare, and it is not often that they lay eggs, so every single egg is precious to us. We must track the bandits back to their camp in the Maishang Hills, recover the chest of turtle eggs, and get them to the Gyala Hatchery before something happens to them. Can you do this for us, <Character name>?"

Notes[edit]

  • Stsou Swiftscale will not spawn if the quest Attack the Kurzicks! is active.
  • It is possible to run this quest without attacking any foes.
