The Undercity is an effectively underground part of Kaineng City, so much has been built above it. It is dominated by the Am Fah, whose guild hall is located here.

Getting there[edit]

Exit from Vizunah Square (Local Quarter), or travel through Wajjun Bazaar or Bukdek Byway.

Shrines[edit]

Resurrection shrines with a statue
  • Avatar of Dwayna
    • Northeast, near the exit to Vizunah Square (Local Quarter)
  • Champion of Balthazar
    • Southwest, near the exit to Wajjun Bazaar
  • Lyssa's Muse
    • Center
  • Melandru's Watcher
    • Southeast
  • Voice of Grenth
    • North, near the exit to Bukdek Byway

Notes[edit]

  • These quests will affect the spawns in this area:
    • Assist the Guards
    • Capturing the Orrian Tome
    • Chasing Zenmai
    • It Ends Here
    • Mayhem in the Market
    • The Challenge
    • The Missing Link
  • When following the arrow and/or star for a quest located in the Undercity, it will direct you to exit from The Marketplace to Wajjun Bazaar.

Trivia[edit]

  • Xaquang Skyway, which is owned by the Jade Brotherhood, is right above the Undercity, which is owned by the Am Fah.
