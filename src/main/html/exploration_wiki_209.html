Magus Stones is the explorable area south and west of Rata Sum. It is populated by Krait and Hylek to the east and west respectively, with deadly spiders roaming the southeast.

Notes[edit]

  • Vanquisher runs of this area without active quests yielded roughly 390 foes or so. Discounting quest influences, it has been reported to range from 368 to 411.
    • The groups of Wind Riders can interrupt most skill usage and apply heavy area of effect damage with Cry of Frustration. Try to spread out before engaging to minimize the shutdown.
    • Beware of hidden Arachnids when you traverse narrow tunnels or valley passes.
    • The density of mobs in the area makes it possible to complete this vanquish while under the effects of a single Hunt Rampage bonus, which would allow one to earn approximately 12,000 Asura reputation points per hour.

Trivia[edit]

  • A Magus is a magician, from the old Persian word for magic.
