Quest information[edit]

Objectives[edit]

  • Travel to Kaineng Center.
  • Talk to Palace Guard Tsungkim in Raisu Pavilion to gain access to Raisu Palace.
  • See Captain Sei for your reward.

Reward[edit]

  • 4,000 Experience
  • 275 Gold
  • 1 Skill Point

Walkthrough[edit]

  1. Map travel to Kaineng Center.
  2. Head through the east portal to Raisu Pavilion.
  3. Speak to Palace Guard Tsungkim to access Raisu Palace.
  4. Speak to Captain Sei for your reward.

Dialogue[edit]

Initial dialogue[edit]

Kuunavang
"The threads of fate are coming together, weaving one with the other. A tapestry is forming, but its patterns are unclear to me.
Master Togo has managed to bring friends and enemies together for an assault on Shiro's army. Join him at Raisu Palace, and look for me in your time of need."
 Accept: "To the palace I go!"
 Decline: "I'm busy right now."
 Ask: "Go with Master Togo and take back the palace. I will be with you."

Reward dialogue[edit]

Captain Sei
"As you can see, the palace has been breached. It is absolute chaos in there. The Afflicted are spreading their sickness to all within, and Shiro has taken the emperor. You are the only hope to save the emperor and, with him, Cantha. We will hold this gate at all costs. Defeat Shiro; we have all suffered too much to fail now."
