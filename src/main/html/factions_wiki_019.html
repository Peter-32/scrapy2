Dragon's Throat is a challenge mission in Kaineng City. It has become infested with the Afflicted and declared a health hazard. Emperor Kisu wants all brave Canthans to fight them.

Mission information[edit]

Objectives[edit]

Kill as many of the afflicted as possible before the timer runs out. Kill an afflicted boss to gain a time bonus.

Reward[edit]

This mission rewards gold based on the formula below. Values are rounded down. Additional bonuses are awarded for beating the daily best, monthly best, or quarterly best scores.

Reward Amount Daily Best Monthly Best Quarterly Best
Gold (500 * score) / (Quarterly Best #1 score) +1500 +15000 +20000

Walkthrough[edit]

Warning: This key section of the article is incomplete. You can help the Guild Wars Wiki by expanding it.

There is a 15 minute time limit for this mission, which is extended by 1 minute for each boss killed. There are two strategies for extending the time limit:

  • To cover long distances quickly, bring party-wide speed boosts, e.g. "Charge!" or "Fall Back!".
  • Remain in the same area and wait for groups to respawn (making sure that there is a boss in the group to extend the time limit).

Notes[edit]

  • As in any challenge mission, foes do not drop items when killed.
  • After 30 minutes players will suffer from Extensive Plague Exposure.
  • The bosses in this mission respawn after being defeated; the death of respawned bosses still adds 1 minute to the mission timer.
