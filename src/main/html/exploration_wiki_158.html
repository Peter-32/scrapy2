Getting there[edit]

Enter the chasm that spawns in Bukdek Byway during the I Feel the Earth Move Under Cantha's Feet quest or after the completion of that quest, What Lies Beneath or Hole of Istan.

Quests available[edit]

  • The Beginning of the End (automatically acquired after the Encounter in the Depths cinematic)

Notes[edit]

  • You can see a room from Bloodstone Caves' second level if you turn around to face the wall behind you when you first start off, then zoom in maximum when against the wall.
  • This is an underground area and does not count towards the Cartographer title
