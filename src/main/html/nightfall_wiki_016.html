Varesh is attempting to turn the once holy site of the Sebelkeh Basilica into a portal for Abaddon's minions. Enter the Basilica and close the torment rifts before Abaddon's The Blasphemy corrupts the Altar of Lyssa.

Mission information[edit]

Requirements[edit]

Tahlkora must be in your party to enter this mission.

Objectives[edit]

The Altar of Lyssa must not be captured by the Blasphemy.

  • Capture the torment rifts guarded by the Margonites. Stand near a rift to claim it. [0...3] of 3 rifts captured.

Walkthrough[edit]

The Sebelkeh Basilica has an octagonal lay out with the Altar of Lyssa at the center and three surrounding platforms to the south, northwest, and northeast. Each platform is initially occupied by 7 Margonites, a Torment Claw, and a Torment Rift. The goal of this mission is to capture all three Torment Rifts without losing the Altar of Lyssa. For each Torment Rift that is captured the first time, your party gains a 2% Morale Boost.

Capturing a rift (or the altar) works the same as similar mechanics in other missions: each character standing on or near the object adds one pip towards capturing it; each foe subtracts a pip; this is capped at a maximum of 4 pips in either direction.

Initial fight[edit]

The party starts standing in a teleportation circle, north from the Altar of Lyssa; leaving the circle starts the countdown timer and triggers the first wave of Margonites: two will rush from each of the three platforms to attempt to capture the Altar. As soon as possible, position party members close to the altar to prevent this from happening.

As soon as you kill the pair of Margonites from a single Rift, a second pair spawns and moves towards the Altar.

The Blasphemy[edit]

The Blasphemy will spawn at a random Rift and move towards the center; to speed up its recapture of the altar, it will use Summon Torment. Kill it to counter this. Unfortunately, its death hexes the entire party with Last Rites of Torment and triggers the spawning of three Unbound Energies; they deal considerable damage to those under the hex.

The Blasphemy continues to respawn throughout the mission; it appears faster with each time you capture a rift. Make sure you kill any remaining Unbound Energies before killing it again. If it heads towards a Rift (to re-open it), send one or more party members to prevent this.

Each time the Blasphemy successfully activates Summon Torment, the rift status bars will fill 25%, allowing the rifts to be reopened and additional margonites to spawn. To prevent this, interrupt the Blasphemy's first use of the skill and kill it before it can cast it again. Keep an eye on the rift bars; if they get to 75%, send a party member to return the rift bar back to blue (or at least, reduce the amount of red).

Capturing the Rifts[edit]

You can ignore The Blasphemy when it first spawns and capture one of the Rifts instead. You must kill all foes in addition to capturing it or they will re-open the Rift later. To speed up the capture, stand near each rift during the fight. Stay spread out while confronting the Torment Claws to avoid massive area of effect damage or take advantage of Torment Slash's long activation time and interrupt it. You can also dodge the slash by moving away.

After capturing the first Rift, go back to the Altar and kill The Blasphemy plus the Unbound Energies. Once you have ensured the safety of the Altar, go capture a second Rift. Then return to kill The Blasphemy again and finally capture the last Rift. The mission will often take a few seconds to complete once all the rifts are closed.

Hard mode[edit]

Successful completion of the mission in hard mode requires being able to kill the initial Margonite groups quickly, otherwise you will get overwhelmed and wipe. Bring either teams that can spike or create overlapping areas of effect (to damage several foes at once).

After clearing out the initial foes from the center, most parties split into two groups: 1-2 characters to hold the altar and the rest to capture the rifts. There are a variety of ways to accomplish this, the most popular of which include a healer with party-wide skills, a minion master, and/or a spirit spammer for the stationary team. The roving team should include mostly damage dealers.

Skill recommendations[edit]

  • Lightbringer's Gaze is a useful interrupt against all enemies, especially the Torment Claws.
  • Wastrel's Worry and Wastrel's Demise are very useful against both the claws and Unbound Energies — the latter do not use any skills and the claws use only one skill with a long activation time.
  • Last Rites of Torment can be removed from the entire party nearly-instantly using Inspired or Revealed Hex. Hex Eater Signet will remove that hex from over half the team, if they are within range.
  • Wards are especially useful, since the party and opposing mobs are restricted to small spaces.
  • With sufficient energy management, Dark Apostasy can work well to remove Spell Breaker.
  • The center altar can be held by two heroes: one useful combination is an Aura of the Lich minion master and a Necromancer/Ritualist healer.
