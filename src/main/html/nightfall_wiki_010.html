Quest information[edit]

Objectives[edit]

  • Travel to Yatendi Canyons and find Kehanni Nutu, priestess of Lyssa.
  • Meet with the Seeker of Whispers.
  • Meet Kehanni in Vehtendi Valley. Obtain an invitation to the Festival of Lyss.
  • Travel through the Forum Highlands. Meet Priestess Haila.
  • Wash the dirt, grime, and heket blood from your armor and gear in the Font of Lyss.
  • Speak with Priestess Haila to gain a sneak peek at the party.
  • See Event Planner Kazsha for your reward.

Reward[edit]

  • 4,000 Experience
  • 400 Gold
  • Trade Contract
  • Trade Contract

Environmental changes[edit]

  • While this quest is active, the bosses Bohdalz the Furious and Jarimiya the Unmerciful do not spawn in Vehtendi Valley.

Walkthrough[edit]

Start in Wehhan Terraces. Follow the path in the map to point 1 and talk to Kehanni. Follow the path to Chantry of Secrets, and talk to the Seeker of Whispers inside (point 2). Follow the path into and through Vehtendi Valley to point 3, where the quest will update. Follow either the green (through Forum Highlands) or the red path (via the Kodash Bazaar) to Priestess Haila outside the Kodash Bazaar. If you wish to enter the Kodash Bazaar, you can also follow the green path and then continue into the Bazaar before talking to the priestess. There you can wash yourself in the Font of Lyss and she will send you into Tihark Orchard.

If you already have the Kodash Bazaar unlocked, you can skip most of this quest by starting from the objective that requires using the Font of Lyss.

Initial dialogue[edit]

Kuwame

"The strangest thing happened just a few moments ago. A devout sister of Lyssa inquired about visitors she was expecting from afar. I told her I hadn't seen anyone and she left. Then out of nowhere you appear! Her name was Kehanni. She said she would be waiting in the Yatendi Canyons. I can only assume she's waiting for you..."
 Accept: "I will seek this priestess and discover what she wants."
 Decline: "Are you sure you haven't been hitting the grog?"
 Ask: "The priestess is waiting for you in Yatendi Canyons. Don't keep her waiting."

Notes[edit]

  • After completion of Tihark Orchard you will find yourself in Kodash Bazaar. The only point in going there before you complete the Tihark Orchard mission is to unlock it a little bit earlier.
  • If you accept the quest reward for this quest with a full inventory, you will not receive the trade contracts and you might not be able to continue the primary quest line.
