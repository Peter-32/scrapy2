The Archipelagos is an area of the Jade Sea that lies south of Cavalon. Due to its proximity to the mainland, this area is riddled with small islands that jut out of the frozen sea. A large settlement of Naga can be found in the south.

Getting there[edit]

Many quests acquired from Cavalon will lead players into and through this explorable area, including the primary quests of Fort Aspenwood, the Jade Quarry, and Stolen Eggs.

This area lies between Cavalon, Breaker Hollow, and Jade Flats (Luxon).

Notes[edit]

  • These quests affect the spawns in this area:
    • Luxon Supply Lines
    • Night Raiders
    • The Jade Arena
  • Vanquisher runs of this area without active quests requires defeating around 174 foes. Including quest influences, it has been reported to range from 156 to 237.
    • Anti-melee skills are effective due to a large number of warriors and assassins in this area.
    • Snapjaw Windshell is in a group with several Rockhide Dragons and Saltspray Dragons, making that group very deadly to unprepared groups that are bunched up.
    • There are five hidden Oni spawn points - one on a clearing east of Snapjaw Windshell, two at the Jade Sea path connecting Breaker Hollow and Cavalon, On the hill north of the Luxon arena, and there is one behind Ssuns, Blessed of Dwayna.
  • Exploring this area will contribute approximately 2.6% towards Canthan Cartographer title.
