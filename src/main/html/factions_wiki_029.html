Quest information[edit]

Objectives[edit]

  • Escort Master Togo to the edge of the Unwaking Waters.
  • Speak with Attis to gain access to the Unwaking Waters.
  • See Elder Oxis for your reward.

Reward[edit]

  • 4,000 Experience
  • 1,250 Luxon Faction
  • 150 Gold
  • 1 Skill Point

Walkthrough[edit]

Travel through Silent Surf from Leviathan Pits to Unwaking Waters. All you need to do is make sure Master Togo survives (else the quest fails and must be restarted). Be alert to the movements of various mobs, including those in the distance, as most have wide patrol routes and can easily join aggro while you're busy dispatching another mob.

You must travel into the depths of the Unwaking Waters, and at the heart of this great whirlpool, find the Harvest Temple, Kuan Jun, which has been imprisoned in jade these two hundred years. There you will obtain the power that will help us send Shiro back to the Underworld."

 Ask: "You must travel to the great whirlpool to meet with Mhenlo and the Kurzicks. There I pray we will find the knowledge to defeat Shiro."
