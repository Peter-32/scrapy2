Ferndale lies southeast of the most prominent of the Great Kurzick Houses, House zu Heltzer. It features a central space that is relatively flat and open compared to the rest of the Echovald Forest. It is currently heavily populated by the Dredge, with Wardens mostly confined to the southern areas. The northwestern slopes are populated by Mantids due to the streams in that area.

Getting there[edit]

All of the primary quests acquired in House zu Heltzer lead through here, including the Defenders of the Forest, Defend Fort Aspenwood, and the Jade Quarry.

Most of the secondary quests from the town will also require the player to travel through this area.

Notes[edit]

  • These quests will affect the spawns in this area:
    • Kurzick Supply Lines (Adds 8 hidden groups of six Luxons; although, they will sometimes spawn even if the quest is not in your Quest Log.)
    • Melodic Gaki Flute
    • Revolt of the Dredge
    • Scouting Ferndale (Adds 4 groups of six Luxons.)
  • There are white mists that cause Stone Spores effect.
  • Exploring this area will contribute approximately 3.6 - 3.7% towards Canthan Cartographer title.
  • Vanquisher runs of this area without active quests requires defeating around 245 foes. Including quest influences, it has been reported to range from 225 to 346.
    • There are several spots on narrow paths that contain small groups of hidden Wardens.
    • There are 5 spots that contain hidden Oni groups (3 per group):
      • On a small rise shared with Wardens at the first crossroads northwest from Lutgardis Conservatory.
      • At a dead-end path near the midpoint between Lutgardis Conservatory and Aspenwood Gate (Kurzick).
      • A short way southeast from Tarnen the Bully's location.
      • Northern side of the central open space.
      • In a corner directly north of Saint Anjeka's Shrine (green area).
