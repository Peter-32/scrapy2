The Shenzun Tunnels is a part of Kaineng City that lies close to ground-level, close to the dirty sewage that drains from other parts of the city. It is likely that the extremely poor would reside here, concentrated mostly in the northern areas and in the center. The Am Fah have a presence here, concentrated mostly on the southwestern and northeastern corners - a result of the Afflicted having taken over the other parts.

Getting there[edit]

The To Tahnnakai Temple primary quest will lead characters through this area for the first time.

Travel from Senji's Corner via the Nahpui Quarter explorable area or Xaquang Skyway. Alternatively, when available leave directly from Tahnnakai Temple or Sunjiang District.

Notes[edit]

  • These quests will affect the spawns in this area:
    • Battle in the Sewers
    • City Under Attack
    • Seeking Shelter
    • To Tahnnakai Temple
  • Vanquisher runs of this area without active quests requires defeating around 240 foes. Including quest influences, it has been reported to range from 180 to 256.
    • 4 additional Afflicted popup when approaching The Afflicted Kam (besides those already visible).
    • Similarly, only 4 foes are visible as you approach The Afflicted Li Yun, after which he and several Afflicted popup (see map).
    • There is an Am Fah group that patrols a long route along the western side and might be missed.
    • 10 Royal Guard allies spawn near Maatu Keep during the quest Battle in the Sewers. However, they are not as intelligent in battle as heroes or henchmen.
  • Sometimes the vanquish won't be complete even after you kill everything in the area. In this case, run to all the portals. Occasionally a group of foes walks through it to disappear and you can get the reward as you approach the portal.
