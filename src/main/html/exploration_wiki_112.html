Farming village in the northeast
Arkjok Ward lies to the south of the more populated areas near the Fortress of Jahai. It is mostly dry but there is fertile farmland towards the northeast. The Kournan military maintain their presence in this area in fortifications built along the river.

Getting there[edit]

The Great Escape is the primary quest that will first lead characters into this area.

Secondary quests that take place in this area include A Little Recon and Drake in a Cage.

Notes[edit]

The Guild Wars 2 Wiki has an article on Arkjok Farmlands.
  • Vanquisher runs of this area without active quests requires defeating around 324 foes. Including quest influences, it has been reported to range from 279 to 368.
    • The A Prescription for Conscription quest in particular will produce a lot more insect spawns.
    • Several hidden groups of Mandragors are located in the southeast area from the Yohlon Haven exit.
  • It is possible to acquire all of the Sunspear bounties before killing any related foe, resulting in about 1,300 Sunspear points for clearing the area. Start from the Jahai Bluffs exit in the north and work through the shrines in a counterclockwise direction until arriving the Mandragor Hunt shrine at the exit into Marga Coast. Turn southeast and head towards the Monster Hunt shrine before turning back to the Yohlon Haven exit for the Insect Hunt bounty.
  • The exit into Sunspear Sanctuary is blocked until the completion of Hunted!
