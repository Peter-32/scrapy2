Bokka Amphitheatre
“

Large theater complex controlled by Prince Bokka the Magnificent. The theater puts on opera, tableaus, pageants, and comedie del arte performances.

— gw.dat

Bokka Amphitheatre is the interior of Prince Bokka's palace. Unless there's currently a show going on, it is usually empty.

Getting there[edit]

The amphitheatre is located inside the palace of the prince in the center of Resplendent Makuun.

Exits[edit]

  • Resplendent Makuun (northwest)

Shrines[edit]

Resurrection shrines with a statue
  • Lyssa's Muse
    • At the exit into Resplendent Makuun.

Notes[edit]

  • The following quests affect the spawns in this area:
    • All's Well That Ends Well
    • Norgu's Nightfall
    • Deactivating N.O.X.
