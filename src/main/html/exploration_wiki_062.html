Silverwood is a lush area of the Maguuma Jungle populated largely by centaurs. A Shining Blade outpost lies near the center of the area, at the Quarrel Falls.

Getting there[edit]

Exit from Quarrel Falls or Bloodstone Fen. Alternatively, the quest Mysterious Message will lead you here from Druid's Overlook.

Shrines[edit]

Resurrection shrines
  • North, near Alari Doubleblade.
  • Northwest, near the exit to Bloodstone Fen.
  • South, near the exit to Quarrel Falls.
  • Southwest, near the exit to Ettin's Back.

Notes[edit]

End of the world past the portal into Ettin's Back.
  • Complete exploration of this area contributes approximately 1.8% to the Tyrian Cartographer title.
    • It is possible to walk through the portal leading to Ettin's Back without zoning into that area. Note that this does not yield otherwise non-explorable parts of the map.
  • Vanquisher runs of this area without active quests require defeating around 210 foes. Including quest influences, it has been reported to range from 185 to 221.
    • There are hidden groups of Life Pods and Moss Scarabs.
    • There is a group of three Maguuma Hunters that are easily missed as they patrol a long route in the northwestern corner of the map, south of Bloodstone Fen.
    • Mind also the lone Wind Rider that patrols the fountain area in the northeast.
Anomaly.Just outside the entrance to Bloodstone Fen, there is an area that appears to keep "foggy" as if unexplored.
