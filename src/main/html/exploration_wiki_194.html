Polymock Glacier
Campaign Eye of the North
Region Far Shiverpeaks
Type Explorable area
Exit(s) None

Polymock Glacier is an area where Polymock matches take place. You are brought here when you challenge certain NPCs to a Polymock match.

NPCs[edit]

  • Wokk (Polymock judge)
  • Polymock opponents (one of them, depending on who you challenged):
    • Fonk
    • Grulhammer Silverfist
    • Hoff
  • Polymock pieces (any 6, depending on which were selected for the Polymock match)

Notes[edit]

  • Polymock Coliseum is where Polymock matches take place in the Tarnished Coast.
  • Polymock Crossing is where Polymock matches take place in the Charr Homelands.
