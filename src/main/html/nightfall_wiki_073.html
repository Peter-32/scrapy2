This is the second quest to acquire Margrid the Sly as a hero. It leads you from Yohlon Haven to Pogahn Passage.

Quest information[edit]

Objectives[edit]

  • Show the Map of Gandara to Jack of Truths in Pogahn Passage.
  • Waylay a squad of Kournan guards and "borrow" their armor.
  • Don the Kournan armor.
  • See Jack of Truths for your reward.

Reward[edit]

  • 2,500 Experience
  • Margrid the Sly joins your party.
  • 200 Gold

Walkthrough[edit]

Just walk from Yohlon Haven to the east through the Arkjok Ward. When walking through the fortress, be careful not to aggro the Acolyte of Grenth. When you reach Pogahn Passage, you will find the doors shut. Just walk north a bit and you will find the entrance. At the other end of Pogahn Passage, you will find Jack of Truths. Follow the quest-marker in Pogahn Passage through the water and out to Gandara. Kill the Kournans and pick up the Sets of Kournan Armor. As soon as you "put them on", the cinematic starts. Talk to Jack of Truths for your reward.

Margrid the Sly

"I am starting to like you. You get into as much trouble as Koss. And if you're eventually going to Vabbi, I might just get my hands on something from the princes' belongings. Tell you what, I'll join your party from here on out...for a cut. Besides, I think we can all agree I'm easier to deal with than that secretive demon hunter.
We need to meet with another one of my crew, the Jack of Truths. He can interpret this map and lead us to the loot...and to Kormir. We'll meet again once you've spoken to Jack. The bottom line is: "No me, no Kormir.""

Notes[edit]

  • Should you abandon the quest, Margrid can be found in Marga Coast just outside Yohlon Haven, where she will offer you the quest again.
  • If you have already been to Pogahn Passage you can travel there directly by map. You can alternatively start in Kodonur Crossroads, but you will have to defeat many more enemies.

Trivia[edit]

  • The Sets of Kournan Armor appears like a NPC and not as a quest item.
  • Margrid the Sly's initial dialogue previously began with "I am starting to like you, Istani." It has since been changed to remove "Istani" from the end of the sentence.
