The Breach is the simple name given to the area north where the Charr broke through the wall and invaded into the rest of Ascalon. Most of the once-green terrain has been scorched dry and the rivers tainted with tar.

Getting there[edit]

Characters will most likely venture into this area following the secondary quest of Supplies for the Duke.

This area lies north of Old Ascalon.

Shrines[edit]

Resurrection shrines
  • Northeast, at the exit into Piken Square.
  • South, at the exit into Old Ascalon.
  • Northwest, at the exit into Diessa Lowlands.

Dialogue[edit]

Upon entering (if the Supplies for the Duke quest has not been completed)

Ascalonian Hunter: Psst. Over here!
Ascalonian Hunter: The Breach is a dangerous place, my friends.
Ascalonian Hunter: Duke Barradin has asked me to watch for people brave enough to cross the wall.
Ascalonian Hunter: Follow me. I'll show you the safest way to Piken Square.

at the bowlshaped area

Ascalonian Hunter: The Charr have been patroling the area regualrly, but so far they've left us alone in Piken.

gaining higher ground

Ascalonian Hunter: It's just up ahead. Not far now.

reaching Piken

Ascalonian Hunter: Well friends, I must report to the duke. Perhaps we will meet again soon.

Notes[edit]

  • These quests will affect the spawns in this area:
    • Charr Reinforcements
    • Hammer and Anvil
    • Supplies for the Duke
  • A group of soldiers that includes an Academy Monk has been reported to randomly spawn near the exit to Old Ascalon. If a minion master is in the party, the Academy Monk can be coaxed into following the party, as she will try to heal the minions.
  • Complete exploration of this area contributes approximately 1.3% to the Tyrian Cartographer title.
  • Vanquisher runs of this area without active quests requires defeating around 130 foes. Including quest influences, it has been reported to range from 100 to 134.
    • There are several hidden Devourer groups concentrated near the Old Ascalon exit.
    • A few hidden Crowns of Thorns can be triggered in the southwestern area, in the slightly greener terrain.

Trivia[edit]

  • The Northlands becomes a small part of this area after the Searing.
