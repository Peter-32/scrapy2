Regent Valley is a lush forest area in Ascalon (pre-Searing). It is where King Doric was crowned at King's Watch, and is a place of both great beauty and great importance to Ascalonians.

Notes[edit]

  • Interactive objects
    • Basket of Apples
    • Healing Spring
    • Hidden Stash
  • You can farm Ale using The Bear Hunters mini-game (about 1 Hunter's Ale per 2 minutes). Depending on the grouping of bears, this may allow you to farm up to 4 ales each run.
  • There are four Red Iris Flowers in the area:
    • By the lake, when entering from Lakeside County
    • In or near the field North West to merchant Vencel
    • In the fields around Statue of Melandru
    • In the fields around The Bear Hunters
