Traveler's Vale is most frequently traversed path in the Northern Shiverpeaks as one attempts to cross the mountainous region. It is currently patrolled by the Stone Summit who are eager to drive off all non-Dwarves from the mountain.

Getting there[edit]

The Way is Blocked is the primary quest that takes the player to this area.

Shrines[edit]

Resurrection shrines
  • Northwest, at the exit into Iron Horse Mine.
  • East, at the exit into Yak's Bend.
  • Southeast, at the exit into Ascalon Foothills.
  • Southwest, at the exit into Borlis Pass.

Notes[edit]

  • Interactive objects
    • Steel Chest
  • Complete exploration of this area contributes approximately 1.0% to the Tyrian Cartographer title.
  • Vanquisher runs of this area without active quests requires defeating around 169 foes. Including quest influences, it has been reported to range from 163 to 191.
    • There are hidden Juniper Barks throughout the area.
    • Enchantment based builds are vulnerable to stripping from the large number of Stone Summit in the area.
    • There are several walking groups of 2 Stone Summit each at the eastern part of the map below Yak's Bend which can be overseen easily because they have a larger patrol circle/area.
  • This is said to be where Nicholas found Professor Yakkington as a calf, before the Searing.
