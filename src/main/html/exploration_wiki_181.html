The Battledepths is the only normal explorable area in the Depths of Tyria. The main chamber is empty until completing all parts of Northern Allies, after which Jalis Ironhammer and his servants appear in the northwestern corner of the map, near the exit to Varajar Fells.

Getting there[edit]

Head southwest from Eye of the North, or head southeast from Olafstead. This area cannot be entered until every character in the party has watched the First Vision cinematic.

Quests available[edit]

  • King Jalis Ironhammer
    • Heart of the Shiverpeaks
  • Orozar Highstone
    • Attack on Jalis's Camp
    • In the Service of Revenge
  • High Priest Alkar
    • Destructive Research
    • The Destroyer Challenge

Notes[edit]

  • In the small corridor leading to the room in southwest, the party can be caught in avalanche.
  • The portal to the Central Transfer Chamber is closed before completing Destruction's Depths.
  • Like most non-Nightfall underground explorable areas, this area cannot be vanquished.
