Quest information[edit]

Objectives[edit]

  • Speak with Danika zu Heltzer
  • Meet Danika zu Heltzer outside the gates of Maatu Keep.
  • Speak with Redemptor Karl.
  • Speak with Lift Operator Shulz.
  • See Acolyte Hanz for your reward.

Reward[edit]

  • 3,000 Experience
  • 150 Gold
  • 1 Skill Point

Walkthrough[edit]

Enter Sunjiang District (explorable area) from Zin Ku Corridor, where Mhenlo will join your party. Follow the quest marker to Danika, fighting Am Fah along the way. Once you have talked to Danika you can map travel to Maatu Keep if you have it unlocked. If not, follow the quest marker to Pongmei Valley, fighting Am Fah and Afflicted along the way; once in Pongmei Valley follow the quest marker, watching out for the popup Ranger boss Meynsang the Sadistic (if you follow the quest marker directly), until you get to Danika and talk to her. After talking to Danika and Redemptor Karl, follow the quest marker to Tanglewood Copse. Once in Arborstone (explorable area) follow the quest marker to Lift Operator Shulz, being careful not to get stuck between the two groups of Wardens. Lift Operator Shulz will take you to Arborstone where you can collect the quest reward.

Dialogue[edit]

Initial dialogue[edit]

Emperor's Voice
We have learned of a growing movement among the Kurzick youth that might prove helpful to our cause. It seems they have been stirred by recent events, having grown tired of watching the world pass them by from the comfort and protection of their grand towers. The emperor wishes you to speak with Danika zu Heltzer, Count zu Heltzer's daughter. She may be more prone to helping us gain the Urn of Saint Viktor than her elders. Seek her out near the Sunjiang District. Mhenlo will accompany you into Kurzick territory. It is Master Togo's hope that he will be able to call in some favors, since he gave the zu Heltzers some assistance many years ago while he was a student at the monastery.
 Accept: "I'll speak with Danika."
 Decline: "I have no time to speak with disgruntled, spoiled youths."
 Ask: "Danika is near the entrance to the Sunjiang District. Commander Jafai's men are trailing her so as to keep her from harm until you can get to her."

Reward dialogue[edit]

Acolyte Hanz

Notes[edit]

  • Meynsang the Sadistic and The Afflicted Huan (Ranger) are along the way in this quest if you wish to cap their skills
  • Mhenlo does not need to survive to complete the quest, it can be completed normally even if he is dead.

Trivia[edit]

  • The corpses of both of Kimi's parents use the male Canthan Guard model.
