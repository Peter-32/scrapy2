Polymock Crossing
Campaign Eye of the North
Region Charr Homelands
Type Explorable area
Exit(s) None

Polymock Crossing is an area where Polymock matches take place. You are brought here when you challenge certain NPCs to a Polymock match.

NPCs[edit]

  • Wokk (Polymock judge)
  • Polymock opponents (one of them, depending on who you challenged):
    • Dune Teardrinker
    • Hoff
  • Polymock pieces (any 6, depending on which was selected for the Polymock match)

Notes[edit]

  • Polymock Glacier is where Polymock matches take place in the Far Shiverpeaks.
  • Polymock Coliseum is where Polymock matches take place in the Tarnished Coast.
Bug. Often, if you spam or even just hit a skill after the the opponent has just drawn his second piece, but before the countdown has completed, your energy bar will maintain a display of nearly-full, however the game will display the message that you have no energy. This has only been observed when fighting Dune Teardrinker

Trivia[edit]

  • This area appears to be the real version of the loading screen in the Charr Homelands.
