Wizard's Folly is a mostly mountainous explorable area in pre-Searing Ascalon. The lower parts of this area are lush with greenery while the higher elevations are covered in snow.

Notes[edit]

  • There is an ice cave near the Wizard's Folly tower filled with patrolling Ice Elementals.
  • Collector Savich has the upgrade Krytan armor gloves for all professions in pre-searing.
  • Though they won't directly attack each other, the Stone Elementals and their Hulking counterparts are affected by the Ice Elemental's spell Frozen Burst.

Trivia[edit]

  • This part of the overworld cannot be explored after the Searing.
