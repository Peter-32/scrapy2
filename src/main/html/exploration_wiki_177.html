Getting there[edit]

From Temple of the Ages, head south through the Black Curtain.

Notes[edit]

  • The only resurrection shrine is at the beginning of the area.
  • Apprentices of Verata will steal your party's minions using Verata's Gaze.
  • Complete exploration of this area contributes approximately 1.1% to the Tyrian Cartographer title.
  • These quests will affect the spawns in this area:
    • The Villainy of Galrath (reduces foes by about 20)
    • Heirlooms of the Mad King (Halloween quest)
  • Vanquisher runs of this area without active quests requires defeating around 190 foes. Including quest influences, it has been reported to range from 156 to 205.
    • Many consider this area more difficult than others due to the Cult of Verata presence. The Eyes of Verata rangers with Choking Gas can be countered by hiding behind cliffs, obstructing their arrows from hitting.
    • If the War in Kryta is active, the area will also include Peacekeepers and White Mantle.
