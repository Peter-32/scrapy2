Bukdek Byway is a densely populated area of Kaineng City, with several levels of housing and confusing passages to navigate. The southeastern corner is controlled by the Am Fah of the Undercity while the Jade Brotherhood holds sway towards the northeast. The Afflicted have some presence in the center of the area.

Getting there[edit]

Factions characters will travel north from the Marketplace through here on their way to Kaineng Center for the first time. Non-Factions characters will be required to leave Kaineng Center and travel through this area as part of the primary quest Welcome to Cantha. Many secondary quests take place here.

  • Shadow's Passage (northeast, only after the completion of the Vizunah Square mission)
  • The Marketplace (southwest)

  • Vizunah Square (Foreign Quarter) (east)

Notes[edit]

  • These quests will affect the spawns in this area:
    • Chasing Zenmai
    • Closer to the Stars
    • The Drunken Master
    • Eliminate the Am Fah
    • Eliminate the Jade Brotherhood
    • Finding The Oracle
    • Missing Daughter
    • The Afflicted Guard
    • Welcome to Cantha
  • Vanquisher runs of this area without active quests require defeating around 135 foes. Including quest influences, it has been reported to range from 1 to 228.
    • If Chasing Zenmai is active, there are several ways you can get credit for vanquishing by killing fewer than 10 foes (depending on your starting outpost), since that quest allows you to enter a section initially devoid of any foes. Party members must not have Eliminate the Am Fah or Eliminate the Jade Brotherhood active.
      • With The Afflicted Guard active, only one kill is needed (start from Kaineng Center).
      • With The Drunken Master active and with The Afflicted Guard inactive; to get credit for the vanquish, you only need to defeat Drinkmaster Tahnu (if you start from Kaineng Center).
      • With neither of those active, take the Missing Daughter, which spawns around 10 foes.
      • Without those three quests active, you can still reduce the kill count to less than 60: approach Bryan from the west, but send only heroes and henchmen to him; do not let any players get within compass range. Defeat the three Jade Brotherhood groups that this triggers and clean-up the remaining mobs on the eastern side of the area (do not venture further west, as this will trigger additional foes, potentially raising the required number to the default).
      • Any party members that have completed Cleansing Bukdek Byway must also take Memories of Purity.
    • Initiate Zei Ri will help you vanquish if you have the quest Cleansing Bukdek Byway (or its hard mode equivalent) active, but only until the last Afflicted is killed.

Trivia[edit]

  • There are at least two ambient animals in the area:
    • A napping cat, curled up near the shrine of Balthazar (southeast exit to the Undercity) that will occasionally flick its tail or stretch itself; it was modeled after Linsey Murdock's pet.
    • A dog, resembling a pug, in a trough up the left staircase after exiting Kaineng Center.
  • The Naked Man could be found here during early beta.
