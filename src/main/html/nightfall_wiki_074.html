Quest information[edit]

Requirements[edit]

  • Elonian (Nightfall) character

Objectives[edit]

  • See First Spear Dehvad for your reward.

Reward[edit]

  • 500 Experience
  • 50 Gold

Dialogue[edit]

Initial dialogue[edit]

First Spear Dehvad

Now that you have learned more about the other professions, it's time to make a choice. Think carefully, now. Once you decide, you won't be able to change your mind for some time.

Which secondary profession would you like to choose? (primary profession omited from list)

I wish to take Mesmer as my secondary profession.
I wish to take Necromancer as my secondary profession.
I wish to take Elementalist as my secondary profession.
I wish to take Monk as my secondary profession.
I wish to take Warrior as my secondary profession.
I wish to take Ranger as my secondary profession.
I wish to take Dervish as my secondary profession.
I wish to take Paragon as my secondary profession.
I am not ready to choose.

Reward dialogue[edit]

First Spear Dehvad

A good choice. It suits you well. You are a lucky person indeed to find your true calling in life.

Notes[edit]

Bug.You can abandon this quest after choosing your secondary and before accepting the quest reward and will get a new choice to choose your secondary. However, this will have no effect — your secondary profession has already been locked.
