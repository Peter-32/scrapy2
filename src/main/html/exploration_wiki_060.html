Consulate
“

Headquarters of the Istani government, used for diplomatic relations with Vabbi and Kourna. The Clanmarshal has his offices here.

— gw.dat

The Consulate, also referred to as the Istani Consulate, appears to be the seat of power of the Istani government. Affairs related to matters of state and security are conducted here, including public hearings and tribunals. Elder Suhl is currently the appointed leader of Istan's Council of Elders and Assistant Hahnna assists him in managing day-to-day affairs.

This is a small enclosed location where several primary quests take place.

Getting there[edit]

From Kamadan, the eastern exit (leading into a building) between the Plains of Jarin exit and the Sun Docks exit leads here. Entering from Consulate Docks is also possible although you first have to unlock that outpost.

Exits[edit]

  • Consulate Docks (east, by talking to Assistant Hahnna but only during the quest the Time is Nigh)
  • Kamadan, Jewel of Istan (west)

Quests available[edit]

  • Elder Suhl
    • To Vabbi!

NPCs[edit]

Allies[edit]

  • 10 Assistant Hahnna
  • 20 Elder Suhl

Notes[edit]

  • The quest-only location known as the Tribunal appears as a replica of this location. That area is only accessible during Trial by Fire.
  • Exploration of this area provides less than .1% toward the Elonian Cartographer title
