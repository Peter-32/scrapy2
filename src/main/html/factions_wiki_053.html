Escort reinforcements to the entrance of Sunjiang District.

Quest information[edit]

Objectives[edit]

  • Meet the Royal Guard in the Shenzun Tunnels.
  • Escort the Royal Guard to Commander Jafai.
  • See Royal Guard Kazuya for your reward.

Reward[edit]

  • 3,000 Experience
  • 150 Gold
  • 1 Skill Point

Walkthrough[edit]

After you've been given the quest, map travel to Tahnnakai Temple or Maatu Keep. You will find a group of 10 Royal Guards outside. Follow the directions and clear the mobs in your way. Make sure you keep at least one Guard alive while traveling to Commander Jafai. If you have succeeded, Commander Jafai will let you into the Sunjiang District outpost. Do not linger around since there will be more groups of Afflicted approaching once you get near the commander.

Dialogue[edit]

Initial dialogue[edit]

Emperor's Hand

The emperor tells me that you are heading to the Sunjiang District to fight Shiro. You are either very brave or very foolhardy. Commander Jafai is holding down the front lines there, but I do not think things are going in our favor. Escort these members of the emperor's Royal Guard to Commander Jafai to help reinforce his position.

 Accept: "Just doing my duty to the emperor."
 Decline: "On second thought, it does sound foolhardy."
 Ask: "I have sent word to Commander Jafai in the Sunjiang District to expect you and the Royal Guard. You should make haste, they really need the reinforcements."
