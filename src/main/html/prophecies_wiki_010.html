Quest information[edit]

Objectives[edit]

  • Go to North Kryta Province and follow the road to the fort in the D'Alessio Seaboard. Once there, report your presence to Mantle Knight Karriya.

Reward[edit]

  • 500 Experience
  • Holy Rod
Fire Dmg: 7-13 (Requires 6 Divine Favor)
Damage +10% (while Health is above 50%)
Energy +3 (while Health is above 50%)

Walkthrough[edit]

Exit Lion's Arch into North Kryta Province and head west to D'Alessio Seaboard. Enter the outpost and talk with Mantle Knight Karriya to complete the quest. It's a short trip, however, a few Fire Imps may cause trouble for low level characters. Bring sufficient healing.

Dialogue[edit]

Initial dialogue[edit]

Armen the Guide

Why, hello, and welcome to Lion's Arch. I see you're another one who's been offered sanctuary here by the grace of the White Mantle justiciars.

The White Mantle rules this kingdom, if you hadn't figured that out. So if you mean to stay here, you'd best report your presence officially. There's a barracks in D'Alessio Seaboard, just west of town.

Talk to Mantle Knight Karriya and he'll sort things out for you.

 Accept: "I'll take care of it."
 Decline: "I'm sorry. I have other things I must do."
 Ask: "Go to North Kryta Province and follow the road to the fort in D'Alessio Seaboard. Once there, report your presence to Mantle Knight Karriya."

Reward dialogue[edit]

Mantle Knight Karriya

Justiciar Toriimo told me about you. It is the will of the Unseen, no doubt, that brings you here, for we are greatly troubled by the rising tide of undead that has beset the countryside.

We need a message sent to the Confessor in the temple to the west, so that he may consider how best to protect these lands. You have witnessed for yourself these undead abominations.

I would like you to travel to the Temple of Tolerance and tell Confessor Dorian of all that you have done and seen. If you are ready to undertake this task, gather together a party and click "Enter Mission." [sic]
