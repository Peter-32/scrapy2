The Maishang Hills is the hilly area that lies to the northeast of the Jade Sea. The hills are populated by Naga and Rot Wallows, while the shores see many Kappa groups.

Getting there[edit]

The Stolen Eggs primary quest will lead characters from Cavalon through this area on the way to Gyala Hatchery.

This area is the northeastern-most area of the Jade Sea region, hence it is initially accessible only via the Archipelagos in the southwest.

Exits[edit]

  • Archipelagos (southwest)
  • Bai Paasu Reach (northwest)
  • Eredon Terrace (northeast)
  • Gyala Hatchery (southeast, inaccessible until completion of Stolen Eggs)

Shrines[edit]

Resurrection shrines with bounties
  • Kurzick Priest → Blessing of the Kurzicks
    • Northeast, at the exit into Eredon Terrace (if under Kurzick control).
    • Northwest, opposite the exit into Bai Paasu Reach (if under Kurzick control).
  • Luxon Priest → Blessing of the Luxons
    • Southeast, at the exit into Gyala Hatchery.
    • Northeast, at the exit into Eredon Terrace (if under Luxon control).
    • Northwest, opposite the exit into Bai Paasu Reach (if under Luxon control).
Resurrection shrines with a statue
  • Avatar of Dwayna
    • Southwest, at the exit into the Archipelagos.
  • Champion of Balthazar
    • Northeast, at the exit into Eredon Terrace (glitched; see notes).
    • Northeast-central, at the foot of a slope where a curving path heads up to a high bridge.
  • Lyssa's Muse
    • Center, below the high bridge.
  • Melandru's Watcher
    • Southeast, at the exit into Gyala Hatchery.
  • Voice of Grenth
    • Northwest, opposite the exit into Bai Paasu Reach.

Notes[edit]

  • These quests affect the spawns in this area:
    • Attack the Kurzicks!
    • Challenge of Strength
    • In the Army (Luxon)
    • Scouting Maishang Hills
    • Stolen Eggs
Bugged statue(s) near Eredon Terrace.
  • If you come here from Cavalon via Archipelagos, note that there is no priest with bounty at the shrine at the Archipelagos exit.
  • Vanquisher runs of this area without active quests requires defeating around 185 foes. Including quest influences, it has been reported to range from 153 to 268.
    • Scouting Maishang Hills spawns Kurzick groups, making it much longer.
    • Enter from one of the three outposts to get the blessing for vanquishing. The shrines nearest the Archipelagos exit have no Kurzick or Luxon Priests.
    • There are 5 hidden Oni groups (each consisting of 3 or 4 of them):
      • The path heading south from Eredon Terrace.
      • The path heading southwest from Eredon Terrace.
      • Southeastern path from the Balthazar shrine.
      • Southwestern path from the Balthazar shrine.
      • The narrow path to the west of the high bridge near the center.
  • When vanquishing, remember to visit both areas of sea; the one near Cavalon has 1 group, while the sea area near Gyala Hatchery has 3.
  • Exploring this area will contribute approximately 3.1% towards Canthan Cartographer title.
Bug.The resurrection shrine near Eredon Terrace has the statues of Dwayna and Balthazar mixed up. Only the Champion of Balthazar can appear.
