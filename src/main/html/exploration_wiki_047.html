The dominant structure in Jahai Bluffs is the formidable Fortress of Jahai. Under the protection of the garrisoned military, this area is relatively heavily populated, with small villages dotting the central, southern, and southeastern parts, as well as a large cliff-side settlement along the waterfall and river to the east.

Getting there[edit]

Exiting via the east exit of the Command Post will bring characters here, most likely while following the primary quest Centaur Blackmail.

Notes[edit]

The Guild Wars 2 Wiki has an article on Jahai Bluffs.
  • Despite having hidden Mandragors spread out in the open areas of this location, Mandragor Hunt is not available here.
  • Complete exploration of this area contributes approximately 3.3% to the Elonian Cartographer title track.
  • Vanquisher runs of this area without active quests requires defeating around 285 foes. Including quest influences, it has been reported to range from 261 to 316.
    • Be aware that the fortress ramps to the northeast contains several large groups of elite Kournan military. Careful pulling is critical. Anti-melee skills and physical damage mitigation is helpful.
  • A huge mountain can be seen when looking west-northwest, it is clear when entering from Kodonur Crossroads. It is probably the mountain seen on the map southwest of The Sulfurous Wastes.
