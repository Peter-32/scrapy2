Quest information[edit]

Objectives[edit]

  • Travel to the rendezvous point on the Jahai Bluffs to discover the origin of the mysterious message.
  • See Whispers Adept for your reward.

Reward[edit]

  • 2,500 Experience
  • 200 Gold
  • 100 Sunspear Promotion Points

Walkthrough[edit]

To obtain this quest talk to Lonai. Then go out to Jahai Bluffs and speak to the Whispers Adept for the reward. Margrid the Sly and the Master of Whispers will each offer a quest. These two quests are mutually exclusive, meaning you can choose only one of them. You now have to decide if you prefer Margrid or the Master of Whispers as a hero until you complete the campaign.

Notes[edit]

  • After accepting the quest reward for this quest, both Master of Whispers and Margrid will offer their follow-up quest. Do note that these quests are mutually exclusive and accepting one will cancel the other. Player characters who accept the quests leading to obtaining one hero will not be able to participate in another PC's mission (although they will be able to participate in the quests) to obtain the other hero until their own mission is complete. Each of their branches eventually lead to your character unlocking the hero you selected.
  • When multiple players are in the party, once a player accepts one of the follow-up quests, the quest exclamation mark will no longer show above that corresponding hero's head. This is just a graphical issue; that hero will still offer their quest to players who have not selected. All players in the party can make their own choice about which quest to take.
