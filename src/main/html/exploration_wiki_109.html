Attack of the Nornbear is an alternate version of Sifhalla, for depicting the Nornbear's attack and history.

NPCs[edit]

Allies[edit]

Norn

  • 20 (20) Jora (party ally)
  • 20 (20) Norn Guard
  • 20 (20) Sif Shadowhunter (party ally)

Foes[edit]

Bosses[edit]

Norn

  • 24 (30) Savage Nornbear

Notes[edit]

  • Only accessible during Tracking the Nornbear.
  • Completing Tracking the Nornbear will return you to Sifhalla.
  • The Jora's Curse cinematic plays here.
