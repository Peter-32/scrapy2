Tears of the Fallen is a small, Bog Skale-infested area in Kryta. The area likely gets its name from the two tear-shaped pools that are found just a short distance from the beach. Small hillside farms can be found on the west side of the lakes.

Getting there[edit]

Travel north from Fishermen's Haven or Sanctum Cay through Stingray Strand, or from Riverside Province through Twin Serpent Lakes.

Shrines[edit]

Resurrection shrines
  • South, at the exit into Stingray Strand.
  • Northeast, at the exit into Talmark Wilderness.

NPCs[edit]

Allies[edit]

Services[edit]

  • 10 (20) Arris Weston (collector for 4 Spiked Crests)
  • 10 (20) Daved Logsdon (collector for 4 Abnormal Seeds)

Foes[edit]

Drakes

  • 20 (26) Grand Drake
  • 18 (25) Lightning Drake

Skales

  • 17 (24) Bog Skale Blighter
  • 18 (25) Bog Skale Icetooth

Notes[edit]

  • Complete exploration of this area contributes approximately 0.3% to the Tyrian Cartographer title.
  • Vanquisher runs of this area without active quests requires defeating around 64 foes. Including quest influences, it has been reported to range from 64 to 72.
    • A group of Bog Skale Icetooths may be difficult to spot in the southwestern corner, across a gap from Arris Weston.

Trivia[edit]

  • This is one of the few areas in Kryta that has no changes during the War in Kryta storyline.
