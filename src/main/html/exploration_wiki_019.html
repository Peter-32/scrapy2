Wajjun Bazaar is a busy and prosperous seafront in central Kaineng. A variety of goods and services are available here, since local imports make their way to the other parts of Kaineng from here. The Am Fah have made inroads from the Undercity into this area and control much of the southern roads and alleys, much to the annoyance of the Jade Brotherhood whose guild hall is on the island in the northwest.

Getting there[edit]

Factions characters will first go through this area during the primary quest A Master's Burden.

Characters from other campaigns will find it easiest to come here via the Marketplace.

Notes[edit]

  • These quests will affect the spawns in this area:
    • Core
      • Deactivating P.O.X.
    • Factions
      • A Master's Burden
      • Assassination Attempt
      • Captured
      • Closer to the Stars
      • Little Lost Bear
      • Mayhem in the Market
      • Seek out Brother Tosai
      • Straight to the Top
      • Street Justice
      • The Search for a Cure
    • Winds of Change
      • Family Matters
      • The Gangs of Kaineng
      • Violence in the Streets
Path shared by several quests
  • Several quests that take place here can be done together at once since the quest givers, walkthrough and reward NPCs are all within the same area: Assassination Attempt, Closer to the Stars, Imperial Assistance, Little Lost Bear, Medical Emergency, Problem Salving and Red Tape.
  • Vanquishing this area without any quest influences requires defeating around 220 foes. Including quest influences, you need to kill between 73 to 271 enemies.
  • It is suggested to access the island of Jade Brotherhood from the central bridge, as the pathing at the other bridges encourages runaway hero AI.
    • The Gangs of Kaineng will increase the total number of foes to around 400.
    • You can reduce the number of foes to approximately 90 and neutralize the Am Fah by completing The Search for a Cure and not completing any of these three quests: Seek out Brother Tosai, Drink from the Chalice of Corruption, or Refuse to Drink.
      • Without one of these in your quest log, you will encounter numerous hidden groups, especially in the narrow pathways to the southeast and the cliffside path in the west.
      • With Seek Out Brother Tosai active, you can also avoid killing any mantids: enter from The Marketplace and target only the Jade Brotherhood in the north.
Anomaly.Some players have reported problems with the kills-counter for vanquishing in this area under some narrow circumstances. If the player kills the mantids first, the counter might show 90% completion even though there are up to 200 more non-popup foes remaining. If the player kills the Jade Brotherhood first, then the Vanquish reward is given without killing the mantids.
