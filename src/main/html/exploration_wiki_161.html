Tasca's Demise is a small area in the Southern Shiverpeaks packed with Stone Summit. Most notably, as soon as you come out of Granite Citadel there is a set of three peaks. There are Stone Summit hiding behind all of them and will popup as soon as you approach.

Foes[edit]

Dwarves (Stone Summit)

  • 24 (26) Stone Summit Carver
  • 24 (26) Summit Giant Herder
  • 24 (26) Stone Summit Ranger
  • 24 (26) Dolyak Master
  • 24 (26) Stone Summit Gnasher
  • 24 (26) Stone Summit Heretic

Elementals (Stone Summit)

  • 24 (26) Siege Ice Golem

Tengu (Avicaras)

  • 24 (26) Avicara Brave
  • 24 (26) Avicara Fierce
  • 24 (26) Avicara Guile
  • 24 (26) Avicara Wise

Notes[edit]

  • Complete exploration of this area contributes approximately 0.5% to the Tyrian Cartographer title.
  • Vanquisher runs of this area without active quests require defeating around 78 to 108 foes.
    • While Defend Droknar's Forge is active, there will be more foes to vanquish. However, you will not get vanquishing credit for the Titans that spawn from other Titans, whom you must also kill.
    • The Avicara near Mineral Springs are hostile to the Stone Summit.
    • Watch out for patrols before engaging the Stone Summit in the camp at the center of the zone.
    • An Avicara near Mineral Springs may spawn behind portal requiring to rezone.
Bug.A Shiverpeak Chest (or Locked Chest in hard mode) may sometimes spawn behind the portal to Mineral Springs.

Trivia[edit]

  • This area is named after Marshall Tasca.
  • This location has had its spawn patterns adjusted to increase the amount of enchantment removal due to intensive bot farming.
