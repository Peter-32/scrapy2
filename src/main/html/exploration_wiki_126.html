Once a beautiful place, Regent Valley was reduced to a barren wasteland during the Searing, its land stripped bare and its rivers befouled. While it was never truly a safe place, Regent Valley is now overrun by monsters and highwaymen, and of course the Charr. King's Watch stands in the middle of Regent Valley, between Fort Ranik and Sardelac Sanitarium.

Getting there[edit]

There are many secondary quests that will lead to this area. It lies to the southeast of Old Ascalon and leads to Fort Ranik in its northeastern corner.

Shrines[edit]

Resurrection shrines
  • Northeast, a short distance south of the exit into Fort Ranik.
  • East, at the exit into Pockmark Flats.
  • Northwest, at the exit into Old Ascalon.
Statue of Melandru
  • Center, south of a bridge, at King's Watch.

Notes[edit]

  • Interactive objects
    • Moa Nests
  • There are many hidden Devourers in this area.
  • If entering from Fort Ranik, a small group of soldiers can be seen engaging either the renegades or a group of Charr.
  • You can obtain a Moa Egg (required to hatch a Black Moa Chick) in the southeastern section, near a large number of Moas.
  • Complete exploration of this area contributes approximately 1.6% to the Tyrian Cartographer title.
  • The Renegade Elementalists have Mind Burn in hard mode, a powerful area of effect spell.
  • Vanquisher runs of this area without active quests requires defeating around 150 foes. Including quest influences, it has been reported to range from 129 to 178.
Bug.The fog in the northwest corner of the map does not uncover for an unknown reason, so it will not look like it is explored on the map; exploring this part will still add to your Cartographer title.
Anomaly.At the exit to Pockmark Flats the shrine on the other side looks destroyed with both arches incomplete but when changing the area the shrine is complete and working.
