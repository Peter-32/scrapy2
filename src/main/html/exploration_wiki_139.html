Ascalon Academy
Campaign Prophecies
Region Ascalon (pre-Searing)
Type Cooperative
Party size 4
Duration Short
Preceded by Ascalon Academy (PvP battle)
Followed by The Krytan Ambassador

The Ascalon Academy is the central training institution of the Ascalon Army. Its main building has two distinct clock towers. Access is gained by talking to Sir Tydus and fulfilling the requirements. This mission takes place immediately after a Player vs Player battle. If there are no other players entering the Ascalon Academy, then you will skip the PvP battle completely, and be treated as if you lost (but you will still be able to do the mission).

After competing in the battle, players are taken to this instance where they must accompany Prince Rurik to defeat the Charr agent Vatlaaw Doomtooth.

Mission information[edit]

Requirements[edit]

  • Tyrian (Prophecies) character
  • Completed primary profession test (elementalist, mesmer, monk, necromancer, ranger, or warrior)
  • Character in pre-Searing
  • Having chosen a secondary profession

Objectives[edit]

  • Listen to the briefing given by Sir Tydus and Prince Rurik.
  • ADDED: Journey to the old bunker with Prince Rurik and defeat Vatlaaw Doomtooth and his minions.

Walkthrough[edit]

Follow Prince Rurik into the cave and kill the Grawl and Vatlaaw Doomtooth. Completing this mission will trigger the Searing and take you to post-Searing Ascalon.

Conditional allies[edit]

Up to three of these henchmen join the player's party if there is an insufficient amount of team members otherwise. If there is only one player in the party (you), then Alesia, Stefan, and Orion will join the party. If the player's profession is a primary or secondary Monk, then Alesia will be replaced by Reyna.

Notes[edit]

  • Mechanically, this area is an explorable area, however it acts the same as a mission.
  • While waiting for the mission to start, your character will be teleported away from any portal you approach.
  • During the briefing, you cannot pass the Ascalonian Guards at the top of the stairs; they will body block you.
  • Once this mission has started, your character will no longer be able to return to pre-Searing Ascalon; if you resign or logout/login, you will restart the mission. Likewise, you cannot fail the mission.
  • While this is still a pre-searing area, the level 4 grawl warriors here are the post-searing version.
Bug.Should Prince Rurik die, the mission will not fail; instead a resurrection notice with a countdown of 3 seconds pops up, with no end effect.
