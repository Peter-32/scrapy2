This article is about the version of the Underworld accessed through Tomb of the Primeval Kings. For other versions of the Underworld, see the Underworld (disambiguation).
The Underworld
Campaign Prophecies
Region The Mists
Type Explorable area
Exit(s) Scarred Earth
Images

Map of The Underworld

Parties must defeat all of the enemies to advance to Scarred Earth.

Getting there[edit]

Approach the "Chaos Rift" in Tomb of the Primeval Kings.

NPCs[edit]

Allies[edit]

  • 20 (20) Acolyte Chigo
  • 20 (20) Acolyte Kaizhan

Foes[edit]

Demons (Torment creatures)

  • 28 (30) Grasp of Insanity
  • 24 (26) Scythe of Chaos

Zombies (Underworld army)

  • 22 (26) Chained Soul

Notes[edit]

  • As with any elite area, there are no resurrection shrines; a party wipe here returns your party to the outpost.
  • To stop bot-farming of this area, all ecto dropping monsters were removed from the first room.
