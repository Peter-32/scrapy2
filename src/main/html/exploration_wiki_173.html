The part of the Echovald Forest known as the Drazach Thicket is different from most of the forest. There's little tree canopy overhead as this area consists of smaller trees and much shrubbery.

Getting there[edit]

The Defenders of the Forest is the primary quest that will lead characters through this area.

Drazach Thicket lies between Saint Anjeka's Shrine, Brauer Academy, and the Eternal Grove.

Exits[edit]

  • Brauer Academy (east; 2 exits)
  • The Eternal Grove (south, but only if all members of the party have completed the Defenders of the Forest)
  • Saint Anjeka's Shrine (northwest)

Shrines[edit]

Resurrection shrines with bounties
  • Kurzick Priest → Blessing of the Kurzicks
    • At the northern exit into Brauer Academy (if under Kurzick control).
    • At the exit into Saint Anjeka's Shrine (if under Kurzick control).
    • At the exit into the Eternal Grove.
  • Luxon Priest → Blessing of the Luxons
    • At the northern exit into Brauer Academy (if under Luxon control).
    • At the exit into Saint Anjeka's Shrine (if under Luxon control).
Resurrection shrines with a statue
  • Avatar of Dwayna
    • Center, west of Brauer Academy, by Ruprecht Brauer.
  • Champion of Balthazar
    • South, at the exit into the Eternal Grove.
  • Lyssa's Muse
    • East, at the north exit into Brauer Academy.
  • Melandru's Watcher
    • Northwest, at the exit into Saint Anjeka's Shrine.

Notes[edit]

  • These quests will affect the spawns in this area:
    • A New Escort (Wardens are replaced by Dragon Mosses near the entrance of Saint Anjeka's Shrine.)
    • Discord Wallow Lyre
    • In the Army Now
    • The Defenders of the Forest
  • Due to their different teams, the Wardens and Mantises will fight each other.
  • Exploring this area will contribute approximately 2.6% towards Canthan Cartographer title.
  • Vanquisher runs of this area without active quests requires defeating around 225 foes. Including quest influences, it has been reported to range from 207 to 281.
    • There are 4 hidden Oni groups:
      • Along the northwestern route moving away from the Eternal Grove.
      • Along the western edge, near the center.
      • A short distance south from Saint Anjeka's Shrine.
      • A short distance northwest from Brauer Academy.
    • There are also two Warden spawns.
    • Occasionally, Chkkr Thousand Tails group doesn't spawn as expected when you enter the bosses range. If this happens, they can instead spawn at Chkkr's location when you spawn either Bezzr Wingstorms or Bazzr Icewings group. If you've been through the whole map while vanquishing and are missing a group, check if this has happened.
