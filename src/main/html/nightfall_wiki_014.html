Quest information[edit]

Requirements[edit]

Zhed Shadowhoof must be in your party.

Objectives[edit]

  • Talk to Kormir in the Command Post to find a way across the Desolation.
  • Travel with Zhed to speak with Mirza about crossing the Desolation.
  • Meet Mirza on the outskirts of the Desolation.
  • Return to Kormir.
  • Meet with Kormir at the monument to Palawa Joko's defeat.
  • Examine the monument to Palawa Joko's defeat.
  • Have Zhed help track Palawa Joko. Take Zhed to speak with Dirah Traptail.
  • See Laph Longmane for your reward.

Reward[edit]

  • 2,500 Experience
  • 200 Gold (if this quest is acquired from Lonai)
  • 250 Gold (if this quest is acquired from Zerai the Learner)
  • 10 Lightbringer Points

Walkthrough[edit]

After accepting the quest from Zerai or Lonai, travel to the Sunspear Sanctuary and exit to the Command Post. Speak with Kormir and head out to Turai's Procession through the nearby portal. Follow the quest marker to Mirza Veldrunner. (Most of the enemies can be avoided or a group of hero/henchmen should be able to handle anything you come across.)

The next quest marker is misleading: it is northwest from your location, but the only way to reach it is to head northeast (see this article's map). Meet up with Mirza in The Sulfurous Wastes, then head back to the Command Post to speak with Kormir again, who will instruct you to meet her in Jahai Bluffs. After she leaves, inspect the monument; this releases Palawa Joko himself. He departs quickly after his dialogue; as Zhed points out, there is no point in following him.

Map travel to the Gate of Desolation, exit into Turai's Procession to find Palawa Joko trapped by Dirah Traptail and his band of centaurs. Speak with Dirah to start the cinematic and speak with Laph Longmane to complete the quest.

Since this quest does not require you to kill any foes, you can complete this by running.

However, no mortal soul can survive the sulfur clouds emanating from the Mouth of Torment; the terrible crater formed when the Five Gods struck down Abaddon. Intermediate dialogue[edit]

Kormir

"You got my message. Good. We need to find a way northwest, through the Desolation, but I fear my time on this world is nearing an end. My nightmares have grown stronger; stronger even than Melonni's. They tear at my soul. I know not how much longer I can hold together. You see, my eyes were...back in Gandara...I...nevermind. I need you to take Zhed and speak with Mirza. See if there is any way to cross the deadly sulfur. I will see what other information Nerashi and the others can dig up."

Mirza Veldrunner

"The Desolation? It is death to step within its sulfur haze, for man and runner alike. Nothing living can pass through. Our people know better. But there was an ancient, undead king; perhaps you have heard of him...Palawa Joko. He enslaved our people to serve the undead ranks in his dark Bone Palace. It is rumored he even tamed the great wurms beneath the Sulfurous Wastes to do his bidding. Meet me in the Desolation and I'll show you."

Notes[edit]

  • This quest can only be acquired from:
    • Lonai if your character has completed Nundu Bay.
    • Zerai the Learner if your character has completed Jennur's Horde.
  • Combine this with Centaur Concerns, which is on the way from Sunspear Sanctuary.
    • You can combine this quest with Mirza's Last Stand, if you have previously completed Centaur Concerns.
  • This quest presents an opportunity to capture elite skills from Sergeant Behnwa and Chiossen, Soothing Breeze on the way through Turai's Procession.
Bug.Palawa Joko sometimes fails to appear when the monument is activated; however, the quest still updates (even though there is no dialogue after Kormir leaves).
Anomaly.If you acquire the quest from Lonai, she will claim to not know where the Sunspear Sanctuary is even though she is standing in it.
Anomaly.The gold reward, some of the dialogue, and quest game link differs depending on which NPC this quest is acquired from.
