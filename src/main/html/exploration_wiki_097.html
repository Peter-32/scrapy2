The Issnur Isles is a stretch of lowlands covered with irrigated farmlands. A fishing village lies on the eastern side of the area and a corsair-infested ruined fort stands tall at the center. Skale prowl the watery areas and insects and attacking plants scurry over the dry patches of land.

Getting there[edit]

This area lies between the outposts of Beknur Harbor and Kodlonu Hamlet. The only way to arrive here for the first time is exiting from Beknur Harbor. The primary quest Isle of the Dead leads through this location.

Notes[edit]

  • This area contains the first boss in the Nightfall campaign that has an elite skill.
  • Complete exploration of this area contributes approximately 2.6% to the Elonian Cartographer title track.
  • Is it possible to explore this area without any foes by entering the quest Assault on Beknur Harbor, and running through the gates from the Beknur Harbor outpost.
  • Vanquisher runs of this area without active quests requires defeating around 342 foes. Including quest influences, it has been reported to range from 339 to 361.
    • Vanquishing this area up to 10 times on an Elonian Support weekly bonus will allow any character to achieve Legendary Spearmarshal.
    • The Skale Blighters will spread disease so condition removal skills will come in handy.
Bug.For non-Nightfall characters (only), there are several quest-related spawns or triggers that are incorrect. These quests cannot be done by non-Nightfall characters yet they affect the instance.
  • Healer Zenwa will always initiate his speech for the First Born quest chain and the many Mourning Villagers will always spawn.
  • Troublesome Flamingos for the quest Flamingo-ing... going... gone. always spawn.
