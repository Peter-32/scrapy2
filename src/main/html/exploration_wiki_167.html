Sparkfly Swamp is an explorable area west of the Southern Shiverpeaks. It's still considered part of the Tarnished Coast.

Notes[edit]

  • Saidra's Ashes can be found in the northwest corner of this area.
  • Vanquisher runs of this area without active quests require defeating around 320 foes. Including quest influences, it has been reported to range from 306 to 353.
    • Some Arachnid groups have additional hidden Arachnids that popup when you engage them. Approaching from the North is highly recommended.
    • Beware of overlapping patrols among the dinosaurs and the Heket.
    • If you accept either Giriff's War or Tekks's War, the associated NPC will follow you as an ally and help with the vanquish.
    • If you have not yet obtained Hayda as a hero, she will join as an ally for the vanquish if you have one of the associated quests and approach her location.
    • The quest Finding Gadd will spawn 3 Inscribed Ettin bosses, providing bonus reputation points for defeating them.
  • In the northwest area approaching the water is a view of a head carved from a mountain, with a waterfall flowing from its mouth.
Anomaly.The Zaishen Vanquish quest indicates that the Sparkfly Swamp is part of the Southern Shiverpeaks.

Trivia[edit]

The Guild Wars 2 Wiki has an article on Sparkfly Swamp.
  • This area is known as Sparkfly Fen in Guild Wars 2.
  • This area is considered to be part of the Tarnished Coast (despite being on the far side of the Sea of Sorrows).
