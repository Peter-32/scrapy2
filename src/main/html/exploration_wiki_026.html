Marga Coast is the southwestern coastline of Kourna, an area of the mainland closest to Istan. Kournan military maintains a fort on a small rise a little ways southwest of the village of Ronjok, home of Melonni.

Getting there[edit]

This area lies northwest from Yohlon Haven, the first outpost of Kourna that characters will arrive at, after completion of the Consulate Docks mission. The next primary quest from there, Hunted!, will lead characters through this area.

Notes[edit]

  • The Kournan military soldiers are of two different levels. Level 16 soldiers are found outside the fort; while level 20 soldiers are found inside or near the fort.
  • Vanquisher runs of this area without active quests requires defeating around 282 foes. Including quest influences, it has been reported to range from 254 to 343.
    • Along the beach to the southeast, there are several groups of hidden Frigid Skale. Proceed along the beach slowly.
    • There are also several hidden insect groups.
    • Vanquishing the area with the Feeding Frenzy quest active can be helpful, since your party will gain the help of Toma, a level 22 drake that can instantly kill enemies with low health.
    • When missing some foes, take a look to the southwest and southeast of the Fortress. Some Kournan groups have a long patrol area.
  • Complete exploration of this area adds approximately 2.9% towards the Elonian Cartographer title; portal jumping allows for an additional 0.1–0.2% leeway (see below).
    • The portal to Sunspear Sanctuary is bugged — it allows heroes to walk through it without bouncing (as they do elsewhere).
