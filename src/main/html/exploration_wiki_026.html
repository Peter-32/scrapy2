This is the first explorable area players will visit in the Far Shiverpeaks, and is the site of the mysterious tower, known as the Eye of the North.

Getting there[edit]

This area can initially be entered from Boreal Station after doing the requisite quest to enter Guild Wars Eye of the North.

Exits[edit]

  • Eye of the North (center)
  • Norrhart Domains (north)
  • Battledepths (center of west side)

Notes[edit]

  • The area outside Boreal Station is a popular place for chest runs or to farm experience (for pets, heroes, your character, or the Survivor title).
    • It is almost completely safe: the Snow Wurms spawn in fixed locations, they do little damage (even in Hard Mode), and they do not attack unless you attack them first.
    • On average, a runner can reach two locked chests in less than two minutes.
  • The portals to the Battledepths and Norrhart Domains are closed until your character uses the Scrying Pool in the Hall of Monuments to view the First Vision cinematic.
  • This area is easier to map if either Service: In Defense of the Eye or Snowball Dominance are active.
  • The Ice Imps and Stonewolves are hostile to each other.
  • Vanquisher runs of this area without active quests require defeating around 150 foes. Including quest influences, it has been reported to range from 141 to 163.
    • The first time you visit this zone, a dialogue occurs between Jora and Sif Shadowhunter, which prevents Tjorvi Throstson from spawning. If you want to get Norn blessing right away, rezone through Boreal Station before continuing.
    • All of the Snow Wurms need to be killed to consider this area vanquished.
    • There are hidden Mountain Aloes in the southern areas, mostly near the Mountain Pinesouls.

Trivia[edit]

  • The dialogue with Evennia was added as part of the May 06, 2010 update.
