The Plains of Jarin are the dusty plains of northeastern Istan. They are mostly flat and dry, but the southeastern part of the Plains of Jarin is more verdant. A variety of sentient plants and insects inhabit this explorable area, together with a large population of skales at the western waterways.

Getting there[edit]

The Plains of Jarin connects Kamadan and three other outposts. Leaving Kamadan for the first time using the northwestern exit will bring players here. A variety of quests available in Kamadan and the Sunspear Great Hall lead to and take place here.

Exits[edit]

  • Caverns Below Kamadan (southeast, via the Fissure, only for those with access to Guild Wars Eye of the North)
  • Champion's Dawn (southwest)
  • Kamadan, Jewel of Istan (southeast)
  • Sunspear Great Hall (center)
  • The Astralarium (northwest)

Notes[edit]

The Guild Wars 2 Wiki has an article on Plains of Jarin.
  • The Juvenile Bladed Termites are passive. There's a large group of them in the western waters. Watch out for the lone hostile Irontooth Drake in their midst.
  • Complete exploration of this area contributes approximately 2.7% to the Elonian Cartographer title.
  • Vanquisher runs of this area without active quests requires defeating around 200 foes. Including quest influences, it has been reported to range from 188 to 214.
    • Be careful with the hidden mandragor groups near Tarom Rockbreaker; the boss itself is part of a hidden group.
    • This area will yield about 1,800 Sunspear Promotion Points with all bounties. When vanquishing with non-Elonian characters this area will offer Istani bounties as long as you are below Sunspear rank 6. A Land of Heroes is the easier method for non-Elonian characters to reach rank 6.
    • If the quest Zaishen Elite is active, you must defeat the two Acolytes to successfully vanquish the area.
    • The festival quest White Mist adds three allies Humbugs to your team.
    • It is possible to have a party larger than four, to a maximum of eight, when vanquishing this area if you speak to Dockmaster Dimedeh. Alternatively, complete the Blacktide Den or Venta Cemetery mission immediately before coming to vanquish this area. Any henchmen you have in your party that are not available in Sunspear Great Hall or Kamadan, Jewel of Istan will be removed from your party when you talk to Dockmaster Dimedeh or finish any of the aforementioned missions; however, heroes (maximum of 3 per player will be teleported) and other players will not be removed from your party.
      • You can also get to Kamadan through the Throne of Secrets after completing Abaddon's Gate; however, this only works for a party of players as all heroes and henchman are removed when entering the Throne of Secrets.
