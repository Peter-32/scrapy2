The Isle of the Nameless is an area dedicated to the introduction of basic game mechanics to new players. It includes some quests which lead you to Zaishen training and afterwards to the Zaishen Challenge and the rest of the Battle Isles. It includes practice targets as well as NPC opponents that are skilled in various different sets of skills.
