Mayhem in the Market sends you around Wajjun Bazaar and the Undercity to collect various Monastery people for Togo's upcoming mission. While doing so, you will get information on the situation on the mainland.

Quest information[edit]

Requirements[edit]

  • Canthan (Factions) character

Objectives[edit]

  • Find Kisai in Wajjun Bazaar.
  • Find Mai.
  • Locate Aeson in the Market and speak with him.
  • Find Yuun and speak with him.
  • Find Lukas and speak with him.
  • Locate and speak with Jinzo and Master Togo in the sewers.
  • Find out what Master Togo learned at the palace. Give him your information.
  • Talk to Guardsman Pei.
  • See Guardsman Pei for your reward.

Reward[edit]

  • 3,000 Experience
  • 150 Gold
  • 1 Skill Point

Walkthrough[edit]

Follow the quest marker on the compass; there are no additional enemies from the quest. However, the Undercity can be difficult for players new to high level areas. Make sure to bring plenty of defensive skills. High-leveled Afflicted patrol the areas, along with Am Fah popups. Some might find it easier to enter the Undercity from Bukdek Byway.

Quests that affect the difficulty

The difficulty and completion time of this quest can be drastically decreased if any party member has the following quests:

  • Seek out Brother Tosai — causes all Am Fah in Wajjun Bazaar to be allies.
  • Chasing Zenmai — reduces enemy spawns in the Undercity after the objective "Travel to the Undercity. Zenmai must survive." has been completed and the current objective is "Stop the Am Fah from creating new afflicted soldiers.".

In contrast, the quest can be made more difficult by including party members who have started Winds of Change: unless those players take Memories of Purity from the Herald of Purity, the Afflicted in the area will easily crush an unprepared team.

Dialogue[edit]

Initial dialogue[edit]

Headmaster Greico
"Master Togo has gone to the palace to see if the emperor has any useful information about the plague. As I am sure you know, the emperor and Master Togo are brothers...Master Togo's mother caught the eye of Emperor Kintah, our current emperor's father. Not many are allowed into the emperor's palace, but he permits Master Togo entry because of their shared blood.
While we wait, I have a task for you. I sent some of the other monastery students out into Wajjun Bazaar to speak with the locals to gather information about this plague. I'd appreciate it if you could find out what they have learned and then report your information to Master Togo. The information may prove invaluable in determining the source of the plague."
 Accept: "I'll go find out what I can."
 Decline: "I'd rather stay here with you on the docks."
 Ask: "Lo Sha and the other headmasters and students are in the Bazaar gathering what information they can about the plague. I'm certain they could use your help."

Notes[edit]

  • Non-Factions characters who have completed Zen Daijun can reach Vizunah Square (Local Quarter) by joining a Factions character doing this quest.
    • This is useful for non-Factions characters for progressing the Cartographer title, access to the Undercity, and starting Vizunah Square with a different choice of henchmen.
    • Note that the Factions character has to start this quest from the very beginning in order to be able to ferry the non-Factions character(s) to Vizunah Square (Local Quarter). However you can safely abandon this quest once you reach that outpost and retake it from Headmaster Greico.
Bug.Factions characters who have not completed all the objectives may join a character who is completing the quest and enter Vizunah Square (Local Quarter), allowing both characters to complete the quest.
