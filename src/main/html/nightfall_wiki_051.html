Quest information[edit]

Requirements[edit]

  • Tyrian (Prophecies) or Canthan (Factions) character

Objectives[edit]

  • Rally the Sunspear Scouts. Brief Elite Scout Zusoh on the situation.
  • See Quarrymaster Bohanna for your reward.

Reward[edit]

  • 1,500 Experience
  • 15 Sunspear Promotion Points

Walkthrough[edit]

A very simple quest where you just need to follow the quest marker out of the Sunspear Great Hall and head towards Champion's Dawn in the southwest. You will meet up with some Sunspears and you just need to talk to them before entering the outpost proper and claiming your reward.

NPCs[edit]

Allies[edit]

Humans (Order of the Sunspears)

  • 3 Elite Scout Zusoh
  • 3 Sunspear Warriors (two of them)

Dialogue[edit]

Initial dialogue[edit]

Castellan Puuba
We must secure Champion's Dawn. The corsairs have landed and plan to take advantage of our war preparations to catch us off guard. They may even have designs on conquering all of Istan. There are even reports of Kournans helping them, as well. Little did they expect we would be able to call upon such great heroes as you, [Character Name].
Kormir has gone ahead to help prepare for the assault. Our main force is already committed to the upcoming war. We need you to rally the Sunspear Scouts and the local masons. Report to Elite Scout Zusoh in the Plains of Jarin and then see Quarrymaster Bohanna for further instructions. We must defend Champion's Dawn, or all might be lost.
 Accept: "By the Five Gods, this dawn shall see a new champion!"
 Decline: "Corsairs? No thanks. Too salty for my taste."
 Ask: "Zusoh is waiting for you in the Plains of Jarin."
Bug.[Character Name] is missed in the Castellan Puuba's dialogue.

Intermediate dialogue[edit]

Elite Scout Zusoh
Yes we have heard the reports. Kormir briefed us about the situation, and we await your further orders before joining the meager defenses. But first, you should understand what we scouts do.
We help protect Istan by issuing bounties on local monsters and other less than savory groups such as the corsairs. You can gain ranks within the Order of the Sunspears by taking these bounties upon yourself. You will have such a bounty during this upcoming battle, so try to kill as many corsairs as possible after you reach Champion's Dawn. For Istan!

Elite Scout Zusoh

Move out to reinforce the Cliffs of Dohjok. (The Sunspears leave for the outpost)

Reward dialogue[edit]

Quarrymaster Bohanna
Kormir awaits your reinforcements upon the Cliffs of Dohjok. You meager few are all that stand between Champion's Dawn and the ravages of a corsair pillaging. I hope the legends that precede you are as solid as this stone. Otherwise, I fear I may not live to see the sunset.
