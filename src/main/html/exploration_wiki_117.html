Linnok Courtyard is a small plateau on the eastern side of Shing Jea Monastery, atop a relatively long flight of stairs. This courtyard also connects to Saoshang Trail, the only route from the western half of Shing Jea Island to Seitung Harbor and the eastern half.

Getting there[edit]

Head up the northeastern flight of stairs in Shing Jea Monastery past a Xunlai Agent.

Many of the early profession-related quests will lead you here to meet with Master Togo.

Exits[edit]

  • Saoshang Trail (East, it is locked for Factions characters until the quest the Road Less Traveled, the actual gate is to the North)
  • Shing Jea Monastery (West)

Shrines[edit]

None.
