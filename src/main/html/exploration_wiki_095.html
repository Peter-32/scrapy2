The Gyala Hatchery explorable area is the stretch of Jade Sea that lies between the capital, Cavalon, and the Luxons' turtle hatchery. It is a somewhat dangerous stretch of land that is littered with Naga and Yeti to the north, while the south is dominated by Fishes, Leviathans, and Luxon Outcasts.

Getting there[edit]

Exit north from Leviathan Pits. Although Gyala Hatchery shares its border with the outpost of the same name, it cannot be reached from there.

Notes[edit]

  • On the western edge (near the area with washed out color on the map) is a huge Irukandji encased in a block of jade, in the center of a Flooded Jade Mine.
  • Complete exploration of this area will yield about 2.8% to the Canthan Cartographer title track.
    • Exploration is best done during the mission as, if you avoid triggering the turtles, you can explore most of the area without having to fight anyone.
  • Vanquisher runs of this area without active quests requires defeating around 195 foes. Including quest influences, it has been reported to range from 183 to 209.
    • The Outcasts, Fish, and Leviathans near the southwestern corner are actually hostile to each other and, if you let them, one group will eventually kill the other.
    • There are four spots where hidden Oni will popup:
      • Two at the large island in the southwestern corner.
      • On the large eastern island, Bahnba Shockfoot and his Yetis are on a narrow path. Three Oni are on the other parallel path.
      • Two more on a spot just north of the previous spot, close to the path of the Naga patrols.
      • Two in the northeastern corner.
    • No foes have hex removal and all are fleshy, so hex spells and minion masters work well here.
