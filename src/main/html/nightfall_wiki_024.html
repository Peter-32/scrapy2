Quest information[edit]

Requirements[edit]

You must have chosen Margrid the Sly during Mysterious Message.

Objectives[edit]

  • Search the Halls of Chokhin with Margrid. Look for information on the Hidden City.
  • Pick up and examine the "Duality of Lyssa."
  • Defeat the library defenses.
  • Talk to Goren.
  • Find Goren in the Mirror of Lyss.
  • Do the magic dance to gain entrance to the Hidden City of Ahdashim. (Use the /dance emote near the spot marked by Goren.)
  • Speak to the Lock of Ahdashim.
  • See Lock of Ahdashim for your reward.

Reward[edit]

  • 2,000 Experience
  • 250 Gold
  • 10 Lightbringer Points

Walkthrough[edit]

Start from the Grand Court of Sebelkeh outpost, and head north west through Mihanu Township into the Holdings of Chokhin. Once there, follow the quest pointer to "explore" for clues, then head into the Halls of Chokhin to pick up and examine the Duality of Lyssa.

Clicking on the Duality will spawn the library defenses in the form of several Djinn, of which the fire elementalist Ruby Djinn are probably the most dangerous and should be defeated first. Since the djinn only spawn when the Duality is examined, your party can setup or prepare skills first. After defeating the library defenses, Goren will direct you to meet him in the Mirror of Lyss explorable zone. Although the conversation between Margrid and Goren is triggered by picking up the book and will suggest the party proceed immediately to the Mirror of Lyss, the player must talk to Goren to update the quest after the conversation and prior to leaving Holdings of Chokhin.

On meeting Goren, perform a magic dance (use the /dance emote) to summon the Lock of Ahdashim and open the way into the Dasha Vestibule outpost to collect your reward.

A cinematic between Varesh and General Morgahn will be triggered after speaking to the Lock of Ahdashim.

Dialogue[edit]

Initial dialogue[edit]

Head Priest Vahmani
"As they did during Palawa Joko's reign, the princes have fled to the Hidden City of Ahdashim. This time, however, Varesh will merely storm the vault and slaughter them. Someone must free them before it is too late. Few people know its location and even fewer know how to gain access through the secret entrance. Without a prince or one of their personal guards, it would take a greedy thief with time and motivation to breach it. That corsair friend of yours should be of some use. Take her with you and search the Halls of Chokhin for clues on the whereabouts of the Hidden City."
 Accept: "A hidden city! That sounds exciting."
 Decline: "I hate research."
 Ask: "Travel to the Holdings of Chokhin to the location of the hidden city."

Notes[edit]

  • The quest pointer will not be present if you do not have the hero Margrid the Sly present in your party.
  • The Vabbian Scout just outside Mihanu Township gives a plant hunt blessing, which is quite useful since the route to the first objective is patrolled by several plant creatures.
  • In Halls of Chokhin, you can also take a quest Botanical Research from Librarian Kahnu just near the Duality of Lyssa.
  • When going to meet Goren to have the secret entrance revealed, Honur Hill is the closest outpost to proceed from, which you will almost certainly have passed through earlier during the All's Well That Ends Well primary quest.
