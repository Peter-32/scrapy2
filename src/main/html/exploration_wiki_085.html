Resplendent Makuun houses the extravagant palace of Prince Bokka the Magnificent. One can find many nobles, artists, and performers within the palace grounds. It was first established and ruled by Amaki Voss, the first nominal "prince" of Vabbi.

Getting there[edit]

This area lies to the southeast of the Mirror of Lyss, past Honur Hill. Characters are most likely to venture here for the first time as part of the primary quest All's Well That Ends Well.

Notes[edit]

  • Complete exploration of this area contributes approximately 2.98% to the Elonian Cartographer title; you can clear an additional 0.03% after exploring Bokka Amphitheatre. (Source: Guild Wiki)
  • These quests affect the spawns in this area:
    • Brains or Brawn
    • Goren's Stuff: Part 1
    • Guard Rescue
    • Summertime for Bokka
    • The Show Must Go On
    • Til Death Do Us Part...
  • Vanquisher runs of this area without active quests requires defeating around 170 foes. Including quest influences, it has been reported to range from 157 to 198.
Anomaly.The exit portal marker for Wilderness of Bahdza is placed too far west on the map.

Trivia[edit]

This area is marked as Resplendent Macuun on the map of Elona in official Guild Wars Nightfall Quick Reference Card.
