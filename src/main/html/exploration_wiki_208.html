Shadow's Passage is a narrow passage below the palace. It only functions as the route between Bukdek Byway and Dragon's Throat.

Getting there[edit]

Exit from Dragon's Throat, or travel through Bukdek Byway

Shrines[edit]

Resurrection shrines with a statue
  • Champion of Balthazar
    • East, near the exit to Dragon's Throat
  • Lyssa's Muse
    • West, near the exit to Bukdek Byway

Notes[edit]

  • These quests will affect the spawns in this area:
    • Finding The Oracle
  • Interactive objects
    • Canthan Chest (chest)
  • This area always contains one chest. When entering the area from Bukdek Byway, the chest most often spawns near Afflicted, otherwise you'll find it near Jade Brotherhood.
  • Vanquisher runs of this area without active quests requires defeating around 14 foes. Including quest influences, it has been reported to range from 6 to 39.
    • If starting from Dragon's Throat it is possible to vanquish only the Afflicted groups, The Afflicted Ako and one small spawn before him, by pulling them closer so you are never within range for the Jade Brotherhood to spawn. This will decrease the number of foes to vanquish to approximately 8.
