The Underworld is the realm of Grenth. Many years ago, the realm was ruled by Dhuum, a cruel and unjust god of death who was eventually overthrown and imprisoned.

When the body dies, the spirit will often move on to the Mists, usually the Underworld - though there are cases where spirits end up elsewhere due to external forces. Here the spirits are judged by Grenth for their actions in life and sent to their final resting place. Recently, this realm has become invaded by demons allying themselves with Dhuum, terrorizing the souls of the dead and sending them to locations they do not belong. The Mad Realm is contained within the Underworld, and the Realm of Torment connects there via the Bone Pits.

The Underworld is regarded as one of the hardest if not the hardest area in Guild Wars. As such is has been dubbed an unofficial 'elite area'. A full run of the area requires the completion of 10 difficult quests, many of which require defending certain NPCs, whose death will result in an instant wipe. After these quests the player is faced with Dhuum himself, making for a difficult and drawn out boss fight, which can put all invested time at risk.

However with great risk comes great reward, as The Underworld is the primary place where Globs of Ectoplasm drop, the primary alternate currency in the game. As a result the area has become very popular with speed clearers, and with farmers of specific sections for ecto drops.

Getting there[edit]

To get to the Underworld, all players of the party must be Ascended or Weh no Su (Elonian characters may instead complete the quest Hunted!). Players must then either use a Passage Scroll to the Underworld (in Embark Beach or any of the following locations below), or pay 1 to the Voice of Grenth at a Statue of Grenth in any of the following locations:

  • Chantry of Secrets
  • Temple of the Ages
  • Zin Ku Corridor
  • Southeast corner of Lornar's Pass

Quests available[edit]

All quests are repeatable by re-entering the Underworld. Quests are in order of progression by indentation.

  • Lost Soul: Clear the Chamber
    • Reaper of the Labyrinth: Escort of Souls
    • Reaper of the Labyrinth: Unwanted Guests
    • Reaper of the Labyrinth: Restoring Grenth's Monuments
      • Reaper of the Bone Pits: Imprisoned Spirits
      • Reaper of the Chaos Planes: The Four Horsemen
      • Reaper of the Forgotten Vale: Wrathful Spirits
      • Reaper of the Ice Wastes: Servants of Grenth
      • Reaper of the Spawning Pools: Terrorweb Queen
      • Reaper of the Twin Serpent Mountains: Demon Assassin
        • King Frozenwind: The Nightman Cometh

Notes[edit]

  • If your party wipes, you will be kicked out of the area. This will also happen if certain allies die.
  • Heroes can enter, but henchmen are not allowed.
  • Successfully completing all of the quests in a single session once will unlock the quest The Nightman Cometh from King Frozenwind. After taking this quest players can go inside the Hall of Judgement. When every player is inside the Hall of Judgement, Dhuum, who resides there, will become aggressive and players can defeat him. Defeating Dhuum will unlock a statue in the Hall of Monuments and allow each party member to open the Underworld Chest once.
  • In Hard mode, foes have a constant seven pips of health regeneration and lowered total health.
  • The Underworld is home to the Black Widow, which can only be found there and only after completing certain quests.
  • This area is popular for farming Globs of Ectoplasm.
  • Approximately 50 Skeletons of Dhuum, which can be farmed throughout the year for Every Bit Helps if you stockpiled Everlasting Mobstoppers, spawn in the Underworld without quests. From quests, there are additional spawns:
    • Unwanted Guests spawns nine Skeletons.
    • Terrorweb Queen spawns six Skeletons.
    • Servants of Grenth spawns nine Skeletons.[verification requested]
    • Imprisoned Spirits spawns three Skeletons.
    • The Four Horsemen spawns four Skeletons. [verification requested]
  • If Gwen is a member of your party, you will see a conversation between her and her mother, Sarah. Yet another dialogue will be triggered if both Gwen and Keiran Thackeray are present.
  • Dhuum will not become active until all players are present and can enter the hall together: be sure to bring skills or items to resurrect any dead party members and ensure that none of the others walk away from the computer too soon.
  • The following Zaishen Quests take place here:
    • Vengeful Aatxe (Zaishen quest)
    • Charged Blackness (Zaishen quest)
    • The Four Horsemen (Zaishen quest)
Anomaly. Allies created by summoning stones are not teleported with the party when talking to a Reaper. This can cause wipes on certain quests. One example is getting the Reaper of the Labyrinth killed during The Unwanted Guests.
Bug.If your party begins the fight with Dhuum before King Frozenwind walks past the gate, he will be stuck outside. About a minute after your party defeats Dhuum, King Frozenwind and the Ice Elementals will spawn inside the Hall of Judgment. Sometimes King Frozenwind will not appear once Dhuum dies and the chest will not appear as a result.

Trivia[edit]

The Guild Wars 2 Wiki has an article on The Underworld.
  • Skeletons of Dhuum were introduced during Halloween 2009 in order to help reduce the rate at which the area is farmed, both by solo and speed clearing farmers.[1]
    • The Skeletons were setup to spawn after completing most of the various quests in the area.
  • The May 21, 2010 game update increased health regeneration and lowered the default health of foes in hard mode.
