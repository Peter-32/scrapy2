Polymock Coliseum
Campaign Eye of the North
Region Tarnished Coast
Type Explorable area
Exit(s) None

Polymock Coliseum is an area where Polymock matches take place. You are brought here when you challenge certain NPCs to a Polymock match.

NPCs[edit]

  • Wokk (Polymock judge)
  • Polymock opponents (one of them, depending on who you challenged):
    • Blarp
    • Hoff
    • Necromancer Volumandus
    • Plurgg
    • Yulma
  • Polymock pieces (any 6, depending on which was selected for the Polymock match)

Notes[edit]

  • Polymock Glacier is where Polymock matches take place in the Far Shiverpeaks.
  • Polymock Crossing is where Polymock matches take place in the Charr Homelands.
