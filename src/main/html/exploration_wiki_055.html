The Dejarin Estate is a fertile tract of land bordered in the south by the fortress of Gandara and in the north by the river Elon, which flows around it. It belonged to the Dejarin family before Varesh Ossa forcefully took it from the current head of the family, Zudash Dejarin, the father of Koss. As a side story in the main Nightfall storyline, your character is given the opportunity to help Zudash reclaim his estate as part of the Dejarin Estate series of quests.

Getting there[edit]

From Yohlon Haven, head east through Arkjok Ward and Pogahn Passage.

Alternatively, from the Sunspear Sanctuary, one can head north into the Command Post then west through Jahai Bluffs and south through Kodonur Crossroads.

Notes[edit]

The Guild Wars 2 Wiki has an article on Dejarin Ruins.
  • The majority of Kournan troops are level 16 (normal mode), but level 20 troops can be found inside the fort and when certain quests are active.
  • Complete exploration of this area contributes approximately 3.2% to the Elonian Cartographer title track.
  • Vanquisher runs of this area without active quests requires defeating around 240 foes. Including quest influences, it has been reported to range from 230 to 268.
    • Due to a large number of foes that inflict deal damage with physical attacks, having some melee counter skills is recommended.
    • The central fort and its surrounding areas contain a large number of Kournan military, some with overlapping patrols. Pull carefully.
    • Podaltur the Angry's Spirit Rift is capable of dealing over 500 damage in one hit.
    • Acolyte of Dwayna can be difficult to take down because of her powerful self heals; bring interrupts or skills that can inflict Daze. She does little damage, except for her critical hits.
Bug.For characters that chose Margrid the Sly over Master of Whispers during the quest Mysterious Message, or who have accepted the quest but not yet begun it, Dehjah and three Water Djinn will always spawn near the northern exit. These NPCs are actually part of the quest Secrets in the Shadow, which can no longer be completed.
Bug.After you have killed every soldier in the central fort's garrison, the Commander on the nearby bridge can still call Kournan Siege "to summon siege fire from any nearby garrisons".
