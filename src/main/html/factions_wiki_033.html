Quest information[edit]

Objectives[edit]

  • Find Master Togo at the city gates.
  • Learn what is going on in the sewers.
  • Speak to the emperor in Raisu Pavilion.

Reward[edit]

  • 3,000 Experience
  • 150 Gold

Walkthrough[edit]

Map travel to Maatu Keep or Tanglewood Copse and exit into Pongmei Valley to meet with Master Togo. After Mhenlo and Togo's dialogue, map or walk to Maatu Keep and exit into Shenzun Tunnels and follow the quest marker to Commander Jafai and speak with him. You will fight many Afflicted and Am Fah along the way.

After speaking with Commander Jafai, map travel to Kaineng Center and exit to Raisu Pavilion in the east. Speak with Emperor Kisu to finish the quest.

Dialogue[edit]

Initial dialogue[edit]

Imperial Herald
I have heard much about you but events are unfolding quickly, and I fear we do not have much time for pleasantries. Master Togo has gone to meet Mhenlo near the gates to Kaineng City. You should rendezvous with them as soon as possible.
 Accept: "I'm on my way."
 Decline: "I have some things to finish first."
 Ask: "Master Togo awaits you at the gates to Kaineng City. Time is wasting!"

Intermediate dialogue[edit]

Master Togo:

"Mhenlo has been telling me about Shiro's activities in the sewers. It appears that he is amassing his forces for some kind of major offensive. I fear for the city, and for all of Cantha, if he is not stopped now."

Brother Mhenlo:

"The emperor has sent a small force into the sewers in an attempt to hold back Shiro and his minions, but I'm afraid that they won't be able to hold out much longer. Whatever Shiro is planning, it's a good guess that he's getting ready to move forward."

Master Togo:

"It is imperative that we find out what his plans are before he puts them into action."

Master Togo

<Character name>, we need you to go down into the sewers and locate the Canthan forces...find out whether they have been able to learn anything about Shiro's plans.
Commander Jafai
These...beasts have been attacking in massive numbers, and my men are dropping like flies. I have orders to hold this position, but as you can see, we are just barely holding them off.

Reward dialogue[edit]

Emperor Kisu
The Canthan Empire has suffered gravely...so many lives lost, so many families torn a part.... [sic] My heart is heavy with the loss.
I understand that you have procured both the Spear of Archemorus and the Urn of Saint Viktor. Master Togo believes that together, these weapons might have the power to bring Shiro to his knees, just as the heroic spirits within them did so many years ago. I pray that he is correct.
Please speak to my attendant known as the Emperor's Hand. He will arrange your passage into the sewers.

Notes[edit]

  • Togo will not appear if you have the primary quest The Count's Daughter in your Quest Log and it has not been completed.
