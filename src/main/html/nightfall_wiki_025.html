Quest information[edit]

Objectives[edit]

  • Gain the rank of Sunspear Captain or Level 17. (For Nightfall characters only.)
  • Rally the Sunspears in Churrhir Fields.
  • Speak with Kormir.
  • Speak with Assistant Hahnna to gain an audience with Elder Suhl.
  • See Raidmarshal Mehdara for your reward.

Reward[edit]

  • 1,500 Experience
  • 125 Gold
  • 15 Sunspear Promotion Points

Walkthrough[edit]

Once you meet the requirements to continue the quest, speak to Second Spear Binah to enter Churrhir Fields. Head to the area on your minimap as marked by the green quest Indicator. Wait for Kormir to rally the troops. After Kormir has completed her speech, speak to her, then Map Travel to Kamadan, Jewel of Istan and enter the Consulate portal. Talk to Assistant Hahnna to begin the cutscene. Talk to Raidmarshal Mehdara for the reward.

Dialogue[edit]

Initial dialogue[edit]

Castellan Puuba

Nightfall characters:

"We have the aid of the wind and water, the blessings of the original Sunspear Guard, a fresh batch of newly-minted Sunspears, and the assistance of heroes from across the sea. Kormir is mustering forces at Churrhir Fields. Once you reach the rank of Sunspear Captain or gain sufficient experience [Level 17], help her rally the troops. Indeed, the time for action is nigh."

Prophecies and Factions characters:

"I must say, your reputation proceeds you, foreigner! I heard it said that you had quite a commanding presence, and I can see those weren't merely rumors. We will need your seasoned leadership for the upcoming assault on Varesh Ossa's fortress of Gandara. Kormir is mustering forces at Churrhir Fields. Help her rally the troops. Indeed, the time for action is nigh."
 Accept: "I'm on my way."
 Decline: "The time for action is later."
 Ask: "Churrhir Fields is just south of town. Meet Kormir and rally the troops."

Reward dialogue[edit]

Raidmarshall Mehdara
We have ships and we have men; we have the will and the means. Nothing can stop Kormir once she puts her mind to it. Notes[edit]

  • This quest is sometimes used to ferry people to Consulate Docks, to help low-level characters get access to max armor and other campaigns.
