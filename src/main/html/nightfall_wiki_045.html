Mission information[edit]

Requirements[edit]

  • Koss must be in your party.

Objectives[edit]

Save Chahbek Village.

  • Destroy the corsairs occupying the village.
  • Sink the corsair ship on the north side of the village.
  • Sink the corsair ship on the south side of the village.
  • *BONUS* Keep as many Sunspear Recruits alive as possible.

Rewards[edit]

The reward for this mission depends on how many Sunspear Recruits are still alive at the end. This tutorial's normal mode rewards are substantially less than that of other missions.

Normal mode Surviving recruits Rewards
Standard 0 - 1 250 XP 20 1 5
Expert's 2 750 XP 30 1 5
Master's 3 1,000 XP 50 1 5
Hard mode Surviving recruits Rewards
Standard 0 - 1 1,000 XP 200 1 50
Expert's 2 2,000 XP 300 1 50
Master's 3 3,000 XP 500 1 50

Walkthrough[edit]

Eliminate all of the corsairs attacking the village, including those on the boats on the right and left sides of the map. You are aided in your efforts by the presence of a level 20 Kormir and a handful of Sunspear recruits.

After killing the corsairs near the village's entrance, you have a choice: follow Kormir's advice and sink the two vessels using Istani Fire Oil fired from the catapults or kill the ship-based corsairs using ranged attacks. If you choose the first method, make your way to the fire oil and carry it to one of the catapults: click once to load the weapon and click again to fire it. Each ship you sink will provide a morale boost. Kill the boss next and then clear out the remainder of the corsairs.

  • You can speed things up by flagging koss and any henchmen to kill corsairs while you sink the ships.
  • The recruits sometimes are slow to engage mobs. If this happens, step closer to the enemy so that foes are within aggro range of the sunspears.

Bonus[edit]

Keep as many of the Sunspear recruits alive as possible. If you confront one group at a time, there is almost no risk to losing any to the corsairs, especially if you sink the ships early.

Hard mode[edit]

In hard mode, there are only two risky spots: attacking the boss (who can manage to kill one of the recruits) and confronting the 3-4 mobs of corsairs in the upper right. However, you can easily succeed with any balanced group that can deal some damage and heal the recruits as well as party members. When engaging groups, take out the Corsair Healers first and any of their spellcasters next. Take advantage of the morale boosts you get for sinking the ships and go after the boss before tackling the overlapping groups.

Skill recommendations[edit]

  • Bring condition removal skills, since nearly all foes inflict some condition.
  • In normal mode, non-boss foes do not use any skills. In hard mode, all foes only use one skill, so you can easily disable them. This makes Wastrel's Demise and Worry effective in both modes.
  • None of the foes carry hex spells or hex removal skills.

Notes[edit]

  • Complete exploration of this area will yield about 0.2%[verification requested] to the Elonian Cartographer title track.
  • The boss (Midshipman Bennis) has Natural Resistance, unlike most in Elona. Consider bringing along Wastrel's Worry for damage as often as you can afford to cast it, or Lightning Surge, for an almost-instant knockdown.
  • It is possible for a new paragon to acquire Harrier's Toss from the boss (instead of waiting to reach the skill trainer that has the skill for unlocking) if they visit their guild hall first and buy a Signet of Capture.
  • After completing this mission, your party will be teleported to Churrhir Fields.

Trivia[edit]

  • Prior to the February 21, 2013 update, you could get on a corsair boat by using a teleport skill, which can add up to 0.1% towards the Elonian Cartographer title. If you stayed on the boat while the catapult facing it was fired, you would find yourself unharmed after the explosion, standing on thin air where the deck used to be. The area can still be explored either during the new character tutorial or by using a minion trick
