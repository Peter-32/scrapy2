The Grand Forum of Vabb of The Kodash Bazaar is under attack! Go and help defend it with the aid of the Djinn.

Quest information[edit]

Requirements[edit]

  • Required heroes: Koss and Melonni.

Objectives[edit]

  • Take Koss and Melonni to speak with Butoh the Bold to prepare the defenses of the Grand Forum of Vabb. (Koss and Melonni must be equipped heroes)
  • Help defend the Grand Forum of Vabb. If it should fall, so will the Kodash Bazaar.
  • Speak with Butoh the Bold.
  • See Zerai the Learner for your reward.

Reward[edit]

  • 2,500 Experience
  • 250 Gold
  • 10 Lightbringer Points

Walkthrough[edit]

Start in The Kodash Bazaar. Head out southwest and walk to Butoh the Bold. After talking to him, five groups of Margonites will spawn one after each other. Don't wait in the center for them to arrive, but head directly toward the first group in the southwest. The Ruby Djinn will help you, therefore it is a good idea to heal them. Proceed with the next groups. Don't worry about Margonites massing in the center, they won't do anything until/unless you're in aggro range. After killing all groups, talk again to Butoh the Bold. Map back to The Kodash Bazaar for your reward.

Dialogue[edit]

Initial dialogue[edit]

Zerai the Learner
"Varesh's crusade west through the Desolation has weakened the barrier between our world and the nightmare realm. Already, many suffer terrible nightmares as Abaddon takes advantage of their fears and secrets. It is true. Nightfall is at hand. No doubt they will try to overtake the Grand Forum of Vabb, and with it the Kodash Bazaar. I have been instructed to use the Djinn of Ahdashim to protect key areas. Go with Koss and Melonni to speak with Butoh the Bold. Tell him the djinn will help protect things. He is a great fighter and will know how best to use them in battle. Will you help us one more time, hero?"
 Accept: "Without a doubt."
 Decline: "I'm sure Vabbi is safe."
 Ask: "Butoh the Bold is in Forum Highlands, just outside the Kodash Bazaar."

Intermediate dialogue[edit]

Butoh the Bold
"Ah, great heroes indeed. These djinn that Zerai sent will come in handy. I have stationed them at the edges of the forum. I fear an attack will come all too soon. Several merchants suffering from terrible nightmares have already fled. I fear they will serve as conduits for Abaddon's forces. Here they come!"
Whenever a Margonite group spawns
Butoh the Bold: "Here they come!"
Butoh the Bold: "Lyssa protect us!"
Butoh the Bold: "They're everywhere!"
Butoh the Bold: "Get behind the Djinn!"

Notes[edit]

  • A paragon with "They're on Fire!" will greatly reduce the damage dealt by the Margonites.
  • If you have the quest Norgu's Nightfall active, Butoh the Bold will not spawn.
